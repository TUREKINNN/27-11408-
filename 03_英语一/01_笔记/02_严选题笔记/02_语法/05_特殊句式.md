# 📚 英语一 · 特殊句式（倒装/强调/省略）

> **建立时间**：2026-06-23
> **说明**：倒装、强调、省略是英语的特殊句式，考研阅读、翻译、写作常考
> **重要性**：★★★★

---

## 🎯 一、倒装句

### 1.1 定义

把句子成分从通常位置（主语在前）移到其他位置（前/后）。

### 1.2 全部倒装（主谓全部倒装）

主语和谓语完全倒装。

#### ① here / there / now / then 开头

- **Here comes** the bus.
- **There goes** the bell.
- **Now comes** your turn.

#### ② 表示方向的副词开头

- **Out rushed** the children.
- **Away flew** the bird.
- **In came** the teacher.

#### ③ 表语前置

- Such **is** the case.（情况就是这样）
- Happy **is** the man who...（幸福的是…）

### 1.3 部分倒装（只倒装助动词 / 系动词 / 情态动词）

#### ① 否定副词/短语置于句首

- **Never** have I seen such a beautiful place.
- **Hardly** did I know what to say.
- **Not only** does he speak English, but also he speaks French.
- **Not until** did he leave that I went home.
- **Neither** will he go, **nor** will I.
- **No sooner** had I arrived than he left.

#### ② only + 状语置于句首

- **Only then** did I understand the importance of English.
- **Only in this way** can you succeed.
- **Only after** he finished his homework did he go to bed.

#### ③ 条件句省略 if

- **Were** I you, I would go.（虚拟语气）
- **Had** I known, I would have come.
- **Should** it rain, I would stay home.

#### ④ so / such... that 句型

- **So** beautiful is the scenery **that** many tourists come.
- **Such** a tall man **is** he that I can't see him.
- = He is **such** a tall man **that** I can't see him.

#### ⑤ as 引导让步状语从句

- **Child as** he is, he knows a lot.（虽然是孩子，他懂得很多）
- **Hard as** he tried, he failed.
- 表语/状语提前 + as + 主语 + 谓语

## 🟢 二、强调句

### 2.1 It is... that... 强调句

#### ① 强调主语

- **It is** I **that** am right.（强调主语 I）

#### ② 强调宾语

- **It is** English **that** he studies.（强调宾语 English）

#### ③ 强调时间

- **It was** yesterday **that** I met him.（强调 yesterday）

#### ④ 强调地点

- **It is** in Beijing **that** he lives.（强调 in Beijing）

#### ⑤ 强调原因

- **It is** because he is ill **that** he didn't come.

### 2.2 It 强调句 vs 主语从句

#### 强调句：去掉 It is... that 后，句子仍完整

- It is **yesterday** that I met him.
- 去掉 → I met him yesterday.（完整）

#### 主语从句：去掉后，句子不完整

- It is **true** that he succeeded.
- 去掉 → He succeeded. （仍完整，但是真强调的是"他成功了"这件事，不是"true"）

### 2.3 其他强调方式

#### ① do / does / did + 动词原形（强调谓语）

- I **do** love you.
- He **did** come, but late.

#### ② 倒装强调

- Never have I seen...（从未见过）
- Only in this way can you...（只有这样才能…）

#### ③ what 引导的感叹句

- **What** a beautiful day it is!
- **What** beautiful flowers (they are)!

#### ④ how 引导的感叹句

- **How** beautiful the flowers are!
- **How** fast he runs!

## 🟡 三、省略句

### 3.1 定义

为了避免重复，省略句子中某些成分。

### 3.2 常见省略

#### ① 省略主语

- (I) Thank you.
- (It) Doesn't matter.

#### ② 省略谓语或谓语部分

- Is he coming? - Yes, he **is** (coming).
- I will go if you **will** (go).

#### ③ 省略宾语

- I have read this book if you haven't **read this book**.

#### ④ 省略不定式

- I asked him to come, but he didn't want **to (come)**.

#### ⑤ 省略从属连词

- I think **(that)** he is right.

#### ⑥ 关系代词/副词

- The man **(whom)** I met is my teacher.
- The place **(where)** I live is small.

#### ⑦ 并列句中的省略

- Some students are good at math, others **(are good at)** English.
- I like English, but he **likes** math.

### 3.3 习惯用法省略

- as... as possible = as... as one can
- if any = if there is any
- if necessary = if it is necessary
- if possible = if it is possible
- if so = if it is so
- if not = if it is not

## 🛠️ 四、特殊句式辨析

### 4.1 倒装 vs 强调

- 倒装：改变语序
- 强调：突出某部分

### 4.2 强调句 vs 主语从句

- 强调句：去掉 It is... that 仍完整
- 主语从句：去掉后意义改变

### 4.3 倒装 vs 省略

- 倒装：调换语序
- 省略：省略成分
