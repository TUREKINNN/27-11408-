## 🧠 4 步分析法（速记）

```
第 1 步：找主干 → 主语 + 谓语 + 宾语
第 2 步：找从句 → that/which/who/when/where/why/how
第 3 步：找非谓语 → to do / doing / done
第 4 步：找特殊结构 → 倒装 / 强调 / 省略 / 插入语
```

## 📝 句子 01：2005 Text 1

**中文释义**：即使是拥有完美基因的人也可能因后天缺乏锻炼而体弱多病，反之亦然。

**英文原句**：
> Even those people who possess perfect genes for fitness may end up in poor physical condition, if they fail to give their bodies adequate exercise.

### 🔍 4 步分析

**① 主干**：people may end up in poor physical condition

**② 从句**：who possess perfect genes for fitness (定语从句修饰 people)
- if they fail to give their bodies adequate exercise (状语从句)

**③ 非谓语**：to give (不定式作 fail 的宾语)

**💡 关键短语/词汇**：end up (最终处于), give sth adequate exercise (给…充分锻炼)
