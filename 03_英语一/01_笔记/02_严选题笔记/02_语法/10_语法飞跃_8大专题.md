## 📘 专题 1：简单句核心（5 大基本句型）

### 1.1 主谓（SV）

**结构**：Subject + Verb (不及物动词)

**例句**：
- Birds fly. （鸟飞。）
- The sun rises. （太阳升起。）
- Time flies. （时光飞逝。）

**常见不及物动词**：rise, fall, die, arrive, come, go, run, fly, swim, sit, stand, lie, work, live, happen, occur, exist, succeed, fail

### 1.2 主谓宾（SVO）

**结构**：Subject + Verb (及物动词) + Object

**例句**：
- I love English. （我爱英语。）
- He plays football. （他踢足球。）
- She studies hard. （她努力学习。）

**常见及物动词**：love, like, hate, want, need, have, take, get, make, do, play, study, learn, teach, know, see, hear, find, think, believe, consider, regard

### 1.3 主谓双宾（SVOO）

**结构**：Subject + Verb + Indirect Object + Direct Object

**例句**：
- He gave me a book. （他给了我一本书。）
- She taught us English. （她教我们英语。）
- He told me a story. （他给我讲了一个故事。）

**双宾动词**：give, show, send, bring, pass, hand, teach, tell, lend, offer, pay, write, read, sing, buy, get, fetch, leave, wish

### 1.4 主谓宾宾补（SVOC）

**结构**：Subject + Verb + Object + Complement

**例句**：
- We made him our monitor. （我们选他当班长。）
- I found the book interesting. （我发现这本书很有趣。）
- He painted the wall white. （他把墙刷成白色。）

**宾补动词**：make, elect, choose, appoint, call, name, find, consider, regard, treat, keep, leave, paint, dye, set, get, have, let

### 1.5 主系表（SVP）

**结构**：Subject + Linking Verb + Predicative

**例句**：
- He is a teacher. （他是老师。）
- She looks beautiful. （她看起来很漂亮。）
- The food tastes delicious. （这食物尝起来很美味。）

**系动词**：be (是), become (变得), get (变得), turn (变得), go (变得), come (变得), grow (变得), fall (变得), seem (似乎), appear (似乎), look (看起来), feel (感觉), sound (听起来), taste (尝起来), smell (闻起来), remain (保持), keep (保持), stay (保持), prove (证明是)

## 📙 专题 2：并列句（7 大并列连词）

### 2.1 并列连词分类

| 类型 | 连词 | 用法 | 例句 |
|:----:|:-----|:-----|:-----|
| **并列** | and, both...and, not only...but also, neither...nor | 顺承/递进 | I like English and math. |
| **转折** | but, yet, while, whereas | 转折/对比 | He is poor but happy. |
| **选择** | or, either...or, whether...or | 选择 | Study hard or fail. |
| **因果** | for, so | 原因/结果 | It rained, so we stayed home. |
| **结果** | so...that, such...that | 结果 | He is so tired that he sleeps. |
| **条件** | or (祈使句 + or + 陈述句) | 条件 | Hurry up, or you'll be late. |
| **递进** | then, besides, moreover, furthermore | 递进 | I like it; besides, it's cheap. |

### 2.2 重要句型

**祈使句 + and/or + 陈述句**：
- Study hard, and you will succeed. （努力学习,你就会成功。）
- Hurry up, or you'll miss the train. （快点,否则你会错过火车。）

**not only...but also... 倒装**：
- Not only does he speak English, but also he speaks French. （他不仅说英语,也说法语。）

## 📕 专题 3：三大从句（详见 [02_三大从句.md](02_三大从句.md)）

### 3.1 速查表

| 类型 | 引导词 | 例句 | 识别方法 |
|:----:|:------|:-----|:---------|
| **定语** | which/that/who/whom/whose/when/where/why/as | The book which I bought is good. | 修饰名词 |
| **主语** | that/whether/what/who | That he will come is certain. | 句首 |
| **宾语** | that/whether/if/wh-/what | I believe that he is right. | 动词后 |
| **表语** | that/what | The fact is that he is wrong. | 系动词后 |
| **同位语** | that | The news that he won surprised us. | 抽象名词后 |
| **状语** | when/if/because/so that/although/as/where/than | When I was young, I was happy. | 修饰动词 |

### 3.2 5 大考点

1. **which/that 区分**：限定性定语从句用 that,非限定性用 which
2. **as 引导定语**：the same...as / such...as / as...as
3. **when/where/why 抽象地点**：the time when / the place where / the reason why
4. **whether/if 区分**：whether...or not 固定搭配
5. **the way 后定语省略**：the way (that/in which) he speaks

## 📗 专题 4：非谓语动词（详见 [04_非谓语动词.md](04_非谓语动词.md)）

### 4.1 4 种非谓语速查

| 形式 | 主动/被动 | 时态/状态 | 否定式 |
|:----:|:---------|:---------|:-------|
| **to do** | 主动 | 将来/未发生 | not to do |
| **doing** | 主动 | 进行/一般 | not doing |
| **done** | 被动 | 完成/被做 | - |
| **having done** | 主动 | 完成 | not having done |

### 4.2 7 大做题口诀

1. **动词不定式**：常表目的、原因、结果、将来
2. **动名词**：常作主语、宾语、介词宾语
3. **现在分词**：常表主动、进行、伴随
4. **过去分词**：常表被动、完成、状态
5. **感官/使役动词后**：sb do (主动)/sb doing (主动进行)/sb done (被动)
6. **不定式/动名词作宾语**：固定搭配需记忆
7. **悬垂分词**：分词的逻辑主语必须与主句主语一致

### 4.3 必背固定搭配

**+ doing**：enjoy, avoid, finish, mind, practice, suggest, consider, imagine, fancy, admit, deny, miss, keep, risk, appreciate, look forward to, be used to, get down to, devote to, stick to, object to

**+ to do**：want, wish, hope, expect, plan, decide, determine, agree, promise, refuse, pretend, fail, manage, offer, learn, seem, appear, tend, choose, afford

**+ doing 或 to do（意义不同）**：
- remember doing (记得做过) / remember to do (记得要做)
- forget doing (忘记做过) / forget to do (忘记要做)
- stop doing (停止做) / stop to do (停下来去做)
- try doing (尝试做) / try to do (努力做)
- regret doing (后悔做过) / regret to do (遗憾要做)
- mean doing (意味着) / mean to do (打算做)
- go on doing (继续做) / go on to do (接着做另一件事)

## 📘 专题 5：时态语态（16 时态）

### 5.1 16 时态总表

| 时间 | 一般 | 进行 | 完成 | 完成进行 |
|:----:|:----:|:----:|:----:|:--------:|
| **现在** | do/does | am/is/are doing | have/has done | have/has been doing |
| **过去** | did | was/were doing | had done | had been doing |
| **将来** | will do | will be doing | will have done | will have been doing |
| **过去将来** | would do | would be doing | would have done | would have been doing |

### 5.2 5 大高频时态

1. **一般现在时**：习惯、客观真理、静态描述
   - Time and tide wait for no man.
2. **一般过去时**：过去发生的动作或状态
   - I studied English yesterday.
3. **现在完成时**：过去发生,影响到现在
   - I have lived here for 10 years.
4. **现在进行时**：说话时正在进行的动作
   - I am reading a book now.
5. **一般将来时**：将要发生的动作
   - I will see you tomorrow.

### 5.3 主动语态变被动语态

**公式**：主动句的宾语 → 被动句的主语 + be + 过去分词 + by + 主动句的主语

**例句**：
- He wrote a letter. → A letter was written by him.
- She teaches us English. → We are taught English by her.

## 📙 专题 6：虚拟语气（3 种类型）

### 6.1 3 大类型速查

| 类型 | 条件从句 | 主句 | 例句 |
|:----:|:---------|:-----|:-----|
| **现在虚拟** | if + 过去式 (be → were) | would/should/could/might + do | If I were you, I would go. |
| **过去虚拟** | if + had done | would/should/could/might + have done | If I had studied, I would have passed. |
| **将来虚拟** | if + 过去式 / were to do / should do | would/should/could/might + do | If it should rain, I would stay. |

### 6.2 特殊虚拟句型

**Wish 后虚拟**：
- I wish I were taller. （现在）
- I wish I had studied harder. （过去）
- I wish I would be taller. （将来）

**If only 虚拟**：
- If only I were rich! （我要是富有就好了！）
- If only I had taken his advice! （我要是听了他的建议就好了！）

**as if/as though 虚拟**：
- He talks as if he knew everything.
- She looks as though she had seen a ghost.

**It is (high) time that 虚拟（用过去式）**：
- It is time that we left. （我们该走了。）
- It is high time that we took action. （我们早该采取行动了。）

**would rather / would sooner 虚拟**：
- I would rather you came tomorrow. （宁愿你明天来。）
- I would sooner you had not told him. （宁愿你没告诉他。）

**suggest / insist / demand / require / order / command 等动词后虚拟（should + do, should 可省）**：
- He suggested that we (should) leave. （他建议我们离开。）
- The boss ordered that the work (should) be finished. （老板下令完成工作。）

## 📕 专题 7：特殊句式（倒装/强调/省略）

### 7.1 倒装句

**全部倒装**（主语和谓语完全倒装）：
- Here comes the bus. （车来了。）
- There goes the bell. （铃响了。）
- In came a man with a white beard. （进来一个白胡子的人。）

**部分倒装**（只把助动词/系动词/情态动词提前）：
- Only by working hard can you succeed. （只有努力工作你才能成功。）
- Not until he came did I leave. （直到他来我才离开。）
- Hardly had I arrived when it began to rain. （我一到就下起了雨。）
- No sooner had I left than the phone rang. （我刚走电话就响了。）
- Never in my life have I seen such a thing. （我一生中从未见过这样的事。）
- Not only does he speak English, but also he speaks French.

**条件句省略 if 的倒装**：
- Were I you, I would go. (= If I were you, I would go.)
- Had I known, I would have called. (= If I had known, I would have called.)
- Should it rain, I would stay. (= If it should rain, I would stay.)

### 7.2 强调句

**It is/was... that/who... 强调句型（强调主语、宾语、状语）**：
- It was yesterday that I met him. （我是昨天遇见他的。）
- It is he who/that helped me. （是他帮助了我。）
- It is in Beijing that he lives. （他住在北京。）

**区分强调句与定语从句**：
- It was at 5 o'clock that he came. （强调句,去掉 It is/was...that 句子仍完整）
- It was 5 o'clock when he came. （主语从句,when 引导主语从句）

### 7.3 省略句

**省略主语**：(祈使句) Open the door. = (You) Open the door.

**省略主语和 be**：(状语从句中) When (you are) in Rome, do as the Romans do.

**省略主语和 do**：(表时间、条件、方式、让步等) While (you are) reading, make notes.

**不定式省略 to**：
- He didn't come, though he promised to (come).
- — Would you like to come? — I'd love to (come).

## 📗 专题 8：主谓一致+情态动词

### 8.1 主谓一致 3 大原则

1. **语法一致**：主语和谓语在语法形式上保持一致
2. **意义一致**：主语和谓语在意义上保持一致
3. **就近原则**：谓语动词和最近的主语保持一致

### 8.2 5 大特殊情况

**1. 不定代词 each/every/no one/someone/anyone/everyone + 单数**：
- Everyone is here. （每个人都在。）

**2. either...or / neither...nor / not only...but also + 复数主语就近一致**：
- Neither he nor I am wrong. （我和他都没错。）
- Either you or he is wrong. （要么你错,要么他错。）

**3. 集体名词（family/class/team/committee）作整体用单数,作个体用复数**：
- My family is a big one. （我家是个大家庭。）
- My family are all fond of music. （我家人都喜欢音乐。）

**4. 数学运算/时间/金钱/距离 + 单数**：
- Two plus two is four. （2+2=4。）
- Three years has passed. （3 年过去了。）

**5. the + 形容词/分词（表一类人）+ 复数**：
- The rich are not always happy. （富人并不总是幸福。）
- The injured were taken to hospital. （伤者被送往医院。）

### 8.3 情态动词 4 大用法

1. **能力**：can / could / be able to
2. **许可**：can / could / may / might
3. **推测**：must / may / might / could / can't / couldn't
4. **必要**：must / have to / should / ought to / need

**推测结构**：
- must (一定) / may (可能) / might (也许) / could (可能)
- can't (不可能) / couldn't (不可能)
- 推测现在: must/may/might + do
- 推测过去: must/may/might + have done
- 推测将来: must/may/might + do

**例句**：
- He must be a teacher. （他现在一定是个老师。）
- He must have left. （他一定已经离开了。）
- He may come tomorrow. （他明天可能来。）
- He can't be at home. （他不可能在家。）
