# 📚 英语一 · 非谓语动词

> **建立时间**：2026-06-23
> **说明**：非谓语动词包括动词不定式、动名词、分词（现在/过去）
> **重要性**：★★★★★（考研英语语法核心）

---

## 🎯 一、4 种非谓语动词总览

| 类型 | 形式 | 功能 | 例句 |
|:-----|:-----|:-----|:-----|
| **动词不定式** | to do | 主/宾/表/定/状/补 | I want **to go**. |
| **动名词** | doing | 主/宾/表/定 | **Reading** is fun. |
| **现在分词** | doing | 定/状/补（主动/进行）| The **running** boy is my son. |
| **过去分词** | done | 定/状/补（被动/完成）| The **broken** window needs repair. |

---

## 🟢 二、动词不定式（to do）

### 2.1 用法

#### ① 作主语

- **To learn** English is important.
- = **It** is important **to learn** English.（it 形式主语）

#### ② 作宾语

- I want **to go** home.
- I hope **to see** you soon.
- 动词 + to do：want, hope, wish, decide, expect, agree, plan, learn, manage, offer, promise, refuse, seem, tend, etc.

#### ③ 作宾语补足语

- I want you **to go**.
- ask/tell/allow/permit/advise/encourage/order/want/expect... + sb. + to do

#### ④ 作表语

- My job is **to teach** English.
- His goal is **to win** the game.

#### ⑤ 作定语

- I have a lot of work **to do**.
- I need something **to eat**.

#### ⑥ 作状语

- **To** pass the exam, he studied hard.（目的）
- I'm glad **to see** you.（原因）
- He is too young **to** go to school.（结果）
- He went home **to** find his parents gone.（结果）

### 2.2 动词不定式 vs 动名词（重要）

| 动词 | 不定式 | 动名词 | 例句 |
|:-----|:-------|:-------|:-----|
| like, love, prefer | to do | doing | I like **reading** / **to read**. |
| start, begin | to do | doing | It started **raining** / **to rain**. |
| continue | to do | doing | He continued **working** / **to work**. |
| hate, dread | to do | doing | I hate **waiting**. |
| intend, plan | to do | - | I intend **to go**. |
| expect, hope | to do | - | I expect **to see** you. |
| promise, agree | to do | - | I promise **to come**. |
| avoid, enjoy | - | doing | I enjoy **reading**. |
| mind, finish | - | doing | I finished **working**. |
| practice, suggest | - | doing | I practice **speaking** English. |
| admit, deny | - | doing | He admitted **stealing**. |
| consider, imagine | - | doing | I considered **going**. |
| keep, keep on | - | doing | He kept **talking**. |
| give up, put off | - | doing | I gave up **smoking**. |
| can't help, can't stand | - | doing | I can't help **laughing**. |

### 2.3 不定式省略 to（不带 to 的不定式）

- 感官动词：see, watch, look at, hear, listen to, feel, notice, observe + sb. + do
  - I saw him **run** away.
- 使役动词：make, let, have + sb. + do
  - Make him **go**.
- had better / would rather / might as well + do
  - You had better **go** now.
- 介词 but / except + do（当前后无动词 do 时）
  - I have nothing to do but **wait**.

## 🟡 三、动名词（doing）

### 3.1 用法

#### ① 作主语

- **Reading** is my hobby.
- = **It** is fun **reading** books.

#### ② 作宾语

- I enjoy **reading**.
- I avoid **eating** fast food.

#### ③ 作表语

- My hobby is **reading**.
- Seeing is **believing**. （眼见为实）

#### ④ 作定语

- a **swimming** pool（游泳池）
- a **parking** lot（停车场）
- a **dining** room（餐厅）
- a **reading** room（阅览室）

#### ⑤ 作介词宾语

- I'm good at **swimming**.
- He is interested in **learning** English.

### 3.2 动名词的复合结构

- **My** coming here is a surprise.
- **The** teacher's coming late made us angry.

### 3.3 动名词的时态和语态

| 形式 | 主动 | 被动 |
|:-----|:-----|:-----|
| **一般** | doing | being done |
| **完成** | having done | having been done |

- I remember **having seen** him before.（我曾见过他）
- He didn't mind **being asked** to speak.（他不介意被邀请发言）

## 🟠 四、分词（doing / done）

### 4.1 现在分词（doing）

#### ① 作定语

- The **running** boy is my son.（跑着的男孩）
- The **singing** girl is beautiful.（唱歌的女孩）
- 表示主动、进行

#### ② 作状语

- **Walking** in the park, I met an old friend.（时间状语）
- **Not knowing** what to do, I called him.（原因状语）
- He sat there, **reading** a book.（伴随状语）
- **Compared with** last year, we have made great progress.（让步/对比）
- 表示主动

#### ③ 作表语

- The movie is **exciting**.（令人兴奋的，主语指物）
- The boy is **excited**.（感到兴奋的，主语指人）
- 现在分词作表语：主语是物，形容词性
- 过去分词作表语：主语是人

#### ④ 作补语

- I saw him **running** in the park.
- I had my hair **cut** yesterday.（让别人剪）

### 4.2 过去分词（done）

#### ① 作定语

- The **broken** window needs to be repaired.
- 表示被动、完成

#### ② 作状语

- **Built** in 1900, the building is very old.
- **Seen** from the hill, the city is beautiful.
- **Moved** by his speech, I decided to help.

#### ③ 作表语

- He is **interested** in music.
- I am **disappointed** at the result.

#### ④ 作补语

- I had my car **stolen** last night.
- I want the work **finished** by tomorrow.

### 4.3 现在分词 vs 过去分词

| 现在分词（doing）| 过去分词（done）|
|:----------------|:----------------|
| 主动 | 被动 |
| 进行 | 完成 |
| 例：The **boring** movie | 例：The **bored** audience |
| （令人无聊的电影）| （感到无聊的观众）|

## 🛠️ 五、非谓语动词判断步骤

### 5.1 三步判断法

#### 第 1 步：判断动词在句子中是否作谓语

- 是 → 时态、语态（谓语动词）
- 否 → 非谓语（to do / doing / done）

#### 第 2 步：判断主被动关系

- 主动 → doing / to do
- 被动 → done / to be done

#### 第 3 步：判断时间关系

- 同时 / 进行 → doing
- 将来 / 目的 → to do
- 之前 / 完成 → done / having done

### 5.2 例题分析

**例句**：The teacher **standing** at the door is my English teacher.

- 第 1 步：standing 不是谓语（已经有 is）→ 非谓语
- 第 2 步：teacher 与 stand 是主动 → 现在分词
- 第 3 步：当时正在 → doing
- 答案：现在分词 standing（主动、进行）

**例句**：The book **written** by him is popular.

- 第 1 步：written 不是谓语（已经有 is）→ 非谓语
- 第 2 步：book 与 write 是被动 → 过去分词
- 第 3 步：已写完 → done
- 答案：过去分词 written（被动、完成）
