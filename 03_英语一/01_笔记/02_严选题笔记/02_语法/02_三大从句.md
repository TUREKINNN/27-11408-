# 📚 英语一 · 三大从句

> **建立时间**：2026-06-23
> **重要性**：★★★★★（考研英语语法核心）
> **说明**：三大从句是阅读长难句的主要构成，掌握后可分析 80% 的长难句

---

## 🎯 一、定语从句（最重要）

### 1.1 定义

在复合句中，修饰名词或代词的从句。

### 1.2 先行词

被定语从句修饰的名词或代词。

### 1.3 关系词分类

| 关系词 | 作用 | 例句 |
|:------|:-----|:-----|
| **who** | 修饰人（主语/宾语）| The man **who** is standing there is my father. |
| **whom** | 修饰人（宾语）| The man **whom** I met is my teacher. |
| **which** | 修饰物（主语/宾语）| The book **which** I bought is interesting. |
| **that** | 修饰人/物 | The book **that** I bought is interesting. |
| **whose** | 修饰人/物的所属 | The man **whose** car was stolen is angry. |
| **when** | 修饰时间 | I remember the day **when** we first met. |
| **where** | 修饰地点 | The house **where** I was born is here. |
| **why** | 修饰原因 | The reason **why** he left is unknown. |

### 1.4 限定性 vs 非限定性

- **限定性定语从句**（无逗号）：The book **that** I bought is good.
- **非限定性定语从句**（有逗号）：The book, **which** I bought yesterday, is good.
  - 注意：非限定性不能用 that

### 1.5 特殊用法

#### ① as 引导的定语从句

- The same... as...：I have the same book **as** you have.
- such... as...：Such people **as** you describe are rare.
- as is known（众所周知）：As is known to all, ...

#### ② but 引导的定语从句

- There is no man but errs.（=There is no man who doesn't err.）

#### ③ 介词 + 关系代词

- The man **with whom** I spoke is my teacher.
- The house **in which** I live is small.

## 🟢 二、名词性从句

### 2.1 定义

在复合句中，充当名词（主语、宾语、表语、同位语）的从句。

### 2.2 主语从句

- **That** he is right is obvious.
- **Whether** he will come is uncertain.
- **What** he said is true.
- **When** he will arrive is unknown.

**It 形式主语**：
- **It** is obvious **that** he is right.
- **It** is known **that** the earth moves around the sun.

### 2.3 宾语从句

- I believe **that** he is honest.
- I wonder **whether/if** he will come.
- He asked **what** I wanted.
- The fact **that** he succeeded surprised us.（同位语从句）

### 2.4 表语从句

- The fact is **that** he didn't come.
- The question is **whether** he can do it.
- That is **what** I want to say.

### 2.5 同位语从句

- The news **that** he won the game is exciting.
- I have no idea **what** he meant.
- The fact **that** he lied is clear.

**同位语从句 vs 定语从句**：
- 同位语从句：that 不可省，说明名词内容
  - The fact **that** he is honest is true.
- 定语从句：that 可省，修饰名词
  - The fact **that** you mentioned is true.

## 🟡 三、状语从句

### 3.1 定义

在复合句中，修饰动词、形容词、副词或整个句子的从句。

### 3.2 9 大状语从句

#### ① 时间状语从句

- **when, while, as, before, after, since, until, as soon as, by the time, the moment**
- When I arrived, he had left.
- While I was cooking, he was watching TV.
- I will call you as soon as I arrive.

#### ② 地点状语从句

- **where, wherever**
- Sit **where** you like.
- Wherever you go, I will follow you.

#### ③ 原因状语从句

- **because, as, since, for, now that, in that**
- I stayed home **because** it was raining.
- **Since** you are tired, let's rest.
- **Now that** you are here, let's start.

#### ④ 目的状语从句

- **so that, in order that, lest, for fear that**
- I studied hard **so that** I could pass the exam.
- He hid it **lest** she should see it.

#### ⑤ 结果状语从句

- **so... that, such... that, so, therefore**
- He is **so** smart **that** everyone likes him.
- It was **such** a hot day **that** we stayed inside.

#### ⑥ 条件状语从句

- **if, unless, as long as, on condition that, provided that, suppose/supposing**
- **If** it rains, I won't go.
- I will help you **provided that** you help me.

#### ⑦ 让步状语从句

- **though, although, even though, even if, as, while, no matter...**
- **Although** he is poor, he is happy.
- **No matter** what you say, I won't change my mind.
- Child **as** he is, he knows a lot.（倒装）

#### ⑧ 比较状语从句

- **as... as..., not as... as..., than, the more... the more...**
- He is **as** tall **as** his father.
- The **more** you practice, **the** better you become.

#### ⑨ 方式状语从句

- **as, as if, as though, the way**
- Do **as** I say.
- He talks **as if** he knew everything.

### 3.3 状语从句的位置

- **句首**：When I arrived, he had left.
- **句中**：The man who lives here is a doctor.
- **句尾**：I will come if I have time.

## 🛠️ 四、特殊句型

### 4.1 It is... that... 强调句

- **It was** yesterday **that** I met him.（强调时间）
- **It is** John **that** broke the window.（强调主语）
- **It is** English **that** he studies.（强调宾语）

### 4.2 what 引导的名词性从句

- **What** he said is true.（=The thing that he said is true）
- 注意：what = the thing that

## 📊 五、三大从句辨析

| 特征 | 定语从句 | 名词性从句 | 状语从句 |
|:-----|:---------|:-----------|:---------|
| **作用** | 修饰名词 | 充当名词成分 | 修饰动词/句子 |
| **位置** | 紧随先行词 | 主/宾/表/同位 | 句首/中/尾 |
| **关系词功能** | 替代先行词 | 引导从句 | 表达逻辑关系 |
| **能否省略** | 可省略（限定性）| 不可省 | 不可省 |
