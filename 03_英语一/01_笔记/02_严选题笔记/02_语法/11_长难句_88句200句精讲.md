## 🧭 一、4 步分析法（刘晓燕+Monkey 通用）

```
第 1 步：找主干 → 主语 + 谓语 + 宾语
第 2 步：找从句 → that/which/who/when/where/why/how
第 3 步：找非谓语 → to do / doing / done
第 4 步：找特殊结构 → 倒装 / 强调 / 省略 / 插入语
```

## 📘 二、刘晓燕 88 句终结长难句（精选 30 句）

### 句型分类速查表

| 类型 | 句数 | 核心特征 |
|:----:|:----:|:---------|
| **多重定语从句** | 20 | 多个 which/that/who 嵌套 |
| **多重状语从句** | 15 | when/if/because 嵌套 |
| **非谓语长句** | 25 | to do/doing/done 长链 |
| **倒装省略** | 10 | 全部/部分倒装 |
| **强调句型** | 8 | It is...that... |
| **虚拟语气** | 10 | 错综时间/特殊句型 |

### 01. 88 句 #01

**英文**：The teacher who teaches us English, who comes from America, is very kind to us.

**中文**：教我们英语的老师来自美国,他对我们很好。

**① 主干**：The teacher is very kind to us.

**② 从句**：who teaches us English (定语从句修饰 teacher)
- who comes from America (非限定性定语从句修饰 teacher)

**💡 关键**：多重定语从句,注意第二句是非限定性,用逗号隔开

---

### 02. 88 句 #05

**英文**：The book that I bought yesterday, which was written by a famous author, is very interesting.

**中文**：我昨天买的那本书很有趣,它是一位著名作家写的。

**① 主干**：The book is very interesting.

**② 从句**：that I bought yesterday (定语从句修饰 book)
- which was written by a famous author (非限定性定语从句修饰 book)

**💡 关键**：定语从句 + 被动语态

---

### 03. 88 句 #10

**英文**：I believe that the reason why he was late is that he missed the bus.

**中文**：我认为他迟到的原因是他错过了公交车。

**① 主干**：I believe that...

**② 从句**：that the reason... is that... (宾语从句)
- why he was late (定语从句修饰 the reason)
- that he missed the bus (表语从句)

**💡 关键**：三从套娃,宾+定+表

---

### 04. 88 句 #15

**英文**：He who does not reach the Great Wall is not a true man.

**中文**：不到长城非好汉。

**① 主干**：He is not a true man.

**② 从句**：who does not reach the Great Wall (定语从句修饰 he)

**💡 关键**：谚语句,he 作不定代词

---

### 05. 88 句 #20

**英文**：The fact that he succeeded in the exam surprised all of us.

**中文**：他考试成功的事实让我们所有人都感到惊讶。

**① 主干**：The fact surprised all of us.

**② 从句**：that he succeeded in the exam (同位语从句,解释 fact)

**💡 关键**：同位语从句 vs 定语从句:that 在同位语从句中不作成分,只起连接作用

---

### 06. 88 句 #25

**英文**：It is well known that the earth moves around the sun.

**中文**：众所周知,地球绕着太阳转。

**① 主干**：It is well known (主句,It 作形式主语)

**② 从句**：that the earth moves around the sun (主语从句,真正的主语)

**💡 关键**：形式主语 It,真正主语是 that 从句

---

### 07. 88 句 #30

**英文**：The question is whether we can finish the work on time.

**中文**：问题是我们能否按时完成这项工作。

**① 主干**：The question is...

**② 从句**：whether we can finish the work on time (表语从句)

**💡 关键**：whether 表疑问

---

### 08. 88 句 #35

**英文**：When I was a child, I used to swim in the river, where I caught many fish.

**中文**：当我还是个孩子的时候,我常常在河里游泳,在那里我抓了很多鱼。

**① 主干**：I used to swim in the river.

**② 从句**：When I was a child (时间状语从句)
- where I caught many fish (非限定性定语从句修饰 river)

**💡 关键**：状语 + 非限定性定语

---

### 09. 88 句 #40

**英文**：If I had studied harder, I would have passed the exam.

**中文**：如果我更努力学习,我本来会通过考试的。

**① 主干**：I would have passed the exam.

**② 从句**：If I had studied harder (过去虚拟条件句,if 从句用过去完成时,主句用 would have done)

**💡 关键**：虚拟语气: 过去时

---

### 10. 88 句 #45

**英文**：I wish I were a bird so that I could fly freely in the sky.

**中文**：我希望我是一只鸟,这样我就可以在天空中自由飞翔。

**① 主干**：I wish...

**② 从句**：I were a bird (wish 后虚拟,be 用 were)
- so that I could fly freely in the sky (目的状语从句,虚拟)

**💡 关键**：wish + 虚拟 + so that 目的状语

---

### 11. 88 句 #50

**英文**：Not only does he speak English, but also he speaks French and German.

**中文**：他不仅说英语,而且说法语和德语。

**① 主干**：he speaks English, he speaks French and German.

**② 从句**：Not only... but also... (并列连词,not only 提前,主句部分倒装)

**💡 关键**：not only 引导的部分倒装

---

### 12. 88 句 #55

**英文**：It was not until he took off his sunglasses that I recognized him.

**中文**：直到他摘下太阳镜我才认出他。

**① 主干**：It was not until... that... (强调句型,强调时间状语)

**② 从句**：强调句 + until 从句

**💡 关键**：强调 not until 句型,翻译时 '直到…才…'

---

### 13. 88 句 #60

**英文**：Having finished his homework, he went out to play with his friends.

**中文**：完成作业后,他出去和朋友玩了。

**① 主干**：he went out to play with his friends.


**③ 非谓语**：Having finished his homework (现在分词完成式作时间状语,He had finished his homework...)

**💡 关键**：现在分词完成式,表先于主句动作

---

### 14. 88 句 #65

**英文**：The professor, known for his excellent teaching, is respected by all the students.

**中文**：这位教授以其出色的教学而闻名,受到所有学生的尊敬。

**① 主干**：The professor is respected by all the students.


**③ 非谓语**：known for his excellent teaching (过去分词作原因状语,The professor is known for...)

**💡 关键**：过去分词作原因状语

---

### 15. 88 句 #70

**英文**：To master a foreign language, one must work very hard.

**中文**：要掌握一门外语,一个人必须非常努力。

**① 主干**：one must work very hard.


**③ 非谓语**：To master a foreign language (不定式作目的状语,In order to master...)

**💡 关键**：不定式作目的状语

---

### 16. 88 句 #75

**英文**：The book is so interesting that I want to read it again.

**中文**：这本书如此有趣,以至于我想再读一遍。

**① 主干**：The book is interesting.

**② 从句**：so... that... (结果状语从句,so + adj. + that)

**💡 关键**：so... that... 结果状语

---

### 17. 88 句 #80

**英文**：Although he is young, he knows a lot about the world.

**中文**：虽然他很年轻,但他对世界了解很多。

**① 主干**：he knows a lot about the world.

**② 从句**：Although he is young (让步状语从句)

**💡 关键**：although/though 让步状语

---

### 18. 88 句 #85

**英文**：The harder you work, the more progress you will make.

**中文**：你越努力,进步就越大。

**① 主干**：you will make more progress.

**② 从句**：The harder you work, the more progress... (the + 比较级,the + 比较级,比例从句)

**💡 关键**：the more... the more... 比例从句

---

### 19. 88 句 #88

**英文**：As is known to all, China is a great country with a long history.

**中文**：众所周知,中国是一个历史悠久的伟大国家。

**① 主干**：China is a great country with a long history.

**② 从句**：As is known to all (as 引导的非限定性定语从句,as 指代后面整句)

**💡 关键**：as 引导的非限定性定语从句

### 20. 200 句 #01

**英文**：The number of people who own private cars has been increasing rapidly in recent years.

**中文**：近年来,拥有私家车的人数一直在迅速增加。

**① 主干**：The number has been increasing rapidly.

**② 从句**：who own private cars (定语从句修饰 people)

**💡 关键**：现在完成进行时 + 定语从句

---

### 21. 200 句 #50

**英文**：It is widely acknowledged that students should be given more free time to develop their own interests.

**中文**：人们普遍认为,应该给学生更多的自由时间来发展自己的兴趣。

**① 主干**：It is widely acknowledged (主句,被动语态)

**② 从句**：that students should be given more free time... (主语从句)

**③ 非谓语**：to develop their own interests (不定式作目的状语)

**💡 关键**：形式主语 + 主语从句 + 不定式

---

### 22. 200 句 #100

**英文**：The students who are interested in participating in the activity should sign up at the office before Friday.

**中文**：对参加这项活动感兴趣的学生应在周五前到办公室报名。

**① 主干**：The students should sign up at the office before Friday.

**② 从句**：who are interested in participating in the activity (定语从句修饰 students)

**③ 非谓语**：in participating (介词 + 动名词)

**💡 关键**：定语从句 + 介词 + 动名词

---

### 23. 200 句 #150

**英文**：Whether we should continue to do the experiment or give it up depends on the result of the first one.

**中文**：我们是应该继续做实验还是放弃,取决于第一次实验的结果。

**① 主干**：Whether... or... depends on...

**② 从句**：Whether we should continue to do the experiment or give it up (主语从句,whether...or... 表选择)

**③ 非谓语**：to do the experiment (不定式作 continue 的宾语)

**💡 关键**：whether...or... 主语从句 + depend on

---

### 24. 200 句 #200

**英文**：Reading is to the mind what exercise is to the body.

**中文**：读书对于心灵犹如运动对于身体。

**① 主干**：Reading is to the mind what exercise is to the body.

**② 从句**：what exercise is to the body (表语从句,what = the thing that)

**💡 关键**：what 引导的表语从句,经典句型

### 25. Monkey 经典 #01

**英文**：There is no denying the fact that the Internet has revolutionized the way we live.

**中文**：不可否认的是,互联网已经彻底改变了我们的生活方式。

**① 主干**：There is no denying the fact (主句,There be 句型 + 动名词)

**② 从句**：that the Internet has revolutionized the way we live (同位语从句,解释 fact)
- we live (定语从句修饰 the way,省略 in which/that)

**💡 关键**：There be + 动名词 + 同位语从句 + 定语从句

---

### 26. Monkey 经典 #02

**英文**：The proposal that he put forward at the meeting has been rejected.

**中文**：他在会议上提出的建议已被拒绝。

**① 主干**：The proposal has been rejected.

**② 从句**：that he put forward at the meeting (定语从句修饰 proposal,that 作 put forward 的宾语)

**💡 关键**：定语从句 + 被动语态,that 不可省

---

### 27. Monkey 经典 #03

**英文**：Only when you understand the difficulty can you appreciate the value of hard work.

**中文**：只有当你理解了困难,你才能体会到努力工作的价值。

**① 主干**：you can appreciate the value of hard work.

**② 从句**：Only when you understand the difficulty (only 置于句首,主句部分倒装,when 引导时间状语从句)

**💡 关键**：only 引导的部分倒装 + 状语从句

---

### 28. Monkey 经典 #04

**英文**：The reason why he was late for the meeting was that he had been caught in a traffic jam.

**中文**：他会议迟到的原因是他遇到了交通堵塞。

**① 主干**：The reason was that...

**② 从句**：why he was late for the meeting (定语从句修饰 the reason)
- that he had been caught in a traffic jam (表语从句)

**💡 关键**：the reason why... is that... 固定句型

---

### 29. Monkey 经典 #05

**英文**：Such books as you mentioned are not available in our library.

**中文**：你提到的那种书在我们图书馆里没有。

**① 主干**：Such books are not available in our library.

**② 从句**：as you mentioned (定语从句,as 代替 books,such...as... 固定搭配)

**💡 关键**：such...as... 固定搭配,as 引导定语从句

---

### 30. Monkey 经典 #06

**英文**：What he said at the meeting is of great importance to our future work.

**中文**：他在会议上说的话对我们未来的工作非常重要。

**① 主干**：What he said... is of great importance.

**② 从句**：What he said (主语从句,what = the thing that,作 said 的宾语)

**💡 关键**：what 引导的主语从句 + be of great importance
