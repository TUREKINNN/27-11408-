# 英语一完形填空技巧

## 一、考查要点总览

完形填空（Cloze Test）共20题，每题0.5分，共10分。考查三大能力：

| 能力 | 占比 | 具体内容 |
|------|------|---------|
| 词汇辨析 | ~45% | 近义词区分、词组搭配、一词多义 |
| 逻辑关系 | ~35% | 连词/副词辨析、段落间逻辑衔接 |
| 语法知识 | ~20% | 时态、语态、非谓语、从句引导词 |

---

## 二、解题步骤：先通读再逐题

### 第一步：通读全文（2-3分钟）

**目标**：把握文章主旨和逻辑走向，不要急于填空。

- 快速浏览首段（通常是主题段）
- 关注每段首句（通常是段落主旨）
- 标记明显的逻辑转折词（but/however/therefore等）

### 第二步：逐题解答（12-15分钟）

**优先级排序**：
1. **先做逻辑关系题**（连词/副词，靠上下文判断）
2. **再做固定搭配题**（介词搭配、动词短语，靠积累）
3. **最后做词汇辨析题**（需要代入语境比较）

### 第三步：回读检查（2-3分钟）

- 通读完整文章，检查语义是否连贯
- 重点检查逻辑词是否正确
- 不确定的题目以第一感觉为准，不要轻易改答案

---

## 三、常考逻辑关系词

### 1. 转折/对比

| 词/短语 | 用法 | 示例 |
|---------|------|------|
| however | 句中/句首转折 | The plan sounds good; **however**, it is impractical. |
| nevertheless | 让步转折，语气更强 | It was raining hard; **nevertheless**, they continued. |
| on the contrary | 完全相反 | He is not lazy; **on the contrary**, he works very hard. |
| whereas / while | 对比 | He likes sports, **whereas** his brother prefers music. |
| yet | 轻微转折 | It is simple **yet** effective. |
| instead | 取而代之 | Don't criticize him; **instead**, offer help. |

### 2. 因果

| 词/短语 | 用法 | 示例 |
|---------|------|------|
| therefore | 因此（正式） | He was injured; **therefore**, he could not play. |
| consequently | 结果 | Prices rose sharply; **consequently**, demand fell. |
| hence | 由此 | The evidence is weak; **hence**, the conclusion is doubtful. |
| thus | 从而 | She studied hard; **thus**, she passed the exam. |
| as a result | 结果 | He ignored the warning; **as a result**, he got lost. |
| due to / owing to | 由于（后接名词） | **Due to** the rain, the match was canceled. |

### 3. 递进/补充

| 词/短语 | 用法 | 示例 |
|---------|------|------|
| furthermore | 此外（正式） | It is cheap; **furthermore**, it is durable. |
| moreover | 而且 | He is talented; **moreover**, he is hardworking. |
| in addition | 另外 | **In addition** to English, she speaks French. |
| what's more | 更重要的是 | The food is good; **what's more**, it is affordable. |
| besides | 除此之外 | **Besides** being a teacher, she is also a writer. |

### 4. 举例/解释

| 词/短语 | 示例 |
|---------|------|
| for example / for instance | Many animals, **for example** pandas, are endangered. |
| that is (to say) | He is a freelancer, **that is**, he works for himself. |
| in other words | The project failed; **in other words**, all efforts were wasted. |
| specifically | Several factors contributed, **specifically** cost and time. |

### 5. 条件/让步

| 词/短语 | 示例 |
|---------|------|
| although / though | **Although** he is young, he is very mature. |
| even though | **Even though** it was expensive, she bought it. |
| provided that | You can go out, **provided that** you finish your homework. |
| as long as | **As long as** you try your best, no one will blame you. |
| unless | He won't succeed **unless** he changes his attitude. |

## 四、高频词汇辨析（10组）

### 第1组：rise / raise / arise / arouse

| 词      | 含义       | 及物/不及物 | 示例                                       |
| ------ | -------- | ------ | ---------------------------------------- |
| rise   | 上升、升起    | 不及物    | The sun **rises** in the east.           |
| raise  | 举起、提高、养育 | 及物     | **Raise** your hand if you agree.        |
| arise  | 出现、产生    | 不及物    | Problems may **arise** from this policy. |
| arouse | 唤起、激起    | 及物     | His speech **aroused** public concern.   |

### 第2组：affect / effect / influence

| 词 | 词性 | 含义 | 示例 |
|----|------|------|------|
| affect | 动词 | 影响 | Climate change **affects** agriculture. |
| effect | 名词 | 效果、影响 | The **effect** of the policy is significant. |
| effect | 动词 | 实现、使生效 | The new mayor **effected** great changes. |
| influence | 动/名 | 潜移默化的影响 | Parents **influence** children's behavior. |

### 第3组：assure / ensure / insure

| 词 | 含义 | 用法 | 示例 |
|----|------|------|------|
| assure | 向……保证 | assure sb. that... | I **assure** you that it is safe. |
| ensure | 确保 | ensure that... | Please **ensure** that all doors are locked. |
| insure | 投保 | insure sth. against... | You should **insure** your house against fire. |

### 第4组：economic / economical

| 词 | 含义 | 示例 |
|----|------|------|
| economic | 经济的（与经济相关） | The **economic** situation is improving. |
| economical | 节约的、省钱的 | This car is very **economical** on fuel. |

### 第5组：considerable / considerate / considering

| 词 | 含义 | 示例 |
|----|------|------|
| considerable | 相当大的 | A **considerable** amount of money was spent. |
| considerate | 体贴的 | She is very **considerate** of others' feelings. |
| considering | 鉴于、考虑到 | **Considering** his age, he is quite healthy. |

### 第6组：adapt / adopt / adept

| 词 | 含义 | 示例 |
|----|------|------|
| adapt | 适应、改编 | You need to **adapt** to the new environment. |
| adopt | 采用、收养 | The company decided to **adopt** a new strategy. |
| adept | 熟练的（adj.） | She is **adept** at solving problems. |

### 第7组：continuous / continual

| 词 | 含义 | 示例 |
|----|------|------|
| continuous | 连续不断的（无间断） | The **continuous** noise kept me awake. |
| continual | 频繁的（有间断） | He suffered from **continual** headaches. |

### 第8组：sensible / sensitive / sensational

| 词 | 含义 | 示例 |
|----|------|------|
| sensible | 明智的、合理的 | That's a **sensible** decision. |
| sensitive | 敏感的 | She is very **sensitive** to criticism. |
| sensational | 轰动的、极好的 | The news was **sensational**. |

### 第9组：respectful / respective / respectable

| 词 | 含义 | 示例 |
|----|------|------|
| respectful | 恭敬的、表示尊敬的 | Be **respectful** to your elders. |
| respective | 各自的 | They went to their **respective** rooms. |
| respectable | 值得尊敬的、体面的 | He is a **respectable** gentleman. |

### 第10组：lie / lay

| 词 | 含义 | 变化 | 示例 |
|----|------|------|------|
| lie | 躺、位于 | lie-lay-lain（不及物） | The book **lay** on the table. |
| lie | 说谎 | lie-lied-lied（不及物） | He **lied** about his age. |
| lay | 放置、下蛋 | lay-laid-laid（及物） | She **laid** the baby on the bed. |

## 五、完形填空高分策略

### 1. 复现原则
文章中常出现**原词复现**或**同义复现**。如果某题不会做，往后读几行，往往能找到答案线索。

### 2. 同现原则
同一话题/场景下的词汇倾向于**同时出现**。例如讨论"教育"话题时，常见词汇：curriculum, faculty, tuition, enrollment, academic等。

### 3. 感情色彩一致
文章的整体感情色彩（褒/贬/中性）保持一致。如果文章在批评某种现象，答案通常也带有负面含义。

### 4. 首尾呼应
首段和末段常有呼应关系，可互相验证。

### 5. 概率统计（当实在不确定时）
考研完形答案分布较均匀，ABCD每个选项通常出现4-5次。如果某选项出现次数明显偏少，可优先考虑。

## 六、每日练习建议

- **基础期**：每天1篇完形真题（计时15-18分钟），重点积累词汇搭配
- **强化期**：每2天1篇，分析每道题的考点类型和干扰项设置
- **冲刺期**：限时训练，15分钟内完成，关注错题率下降趋势
