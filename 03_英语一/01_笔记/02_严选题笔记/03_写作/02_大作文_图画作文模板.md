# 📚 英语一 · 大作文图画作文模板

> **建立时间**：2026-06-23
> **目标**：大作文 14-16 分（满分 20 分）
> **说明**：2010-2024 主要考图画作文，重点掌握三段式

---

## 🎯 一、图画作文 5 大类型

### 1.1 哲理类（最多）

- 主题：人生哲理（坚持、合作、创新、诚信）
- 例子：2010 文化建设（"文化火锅"）、2011 旅程与环境、2012 乐观与悲观
- 寓意：发掘图片背后的人生道理

### 1.2 社会类

- 主题：社会现象（老龄化、网络、诚信、就业）
- 例子：2013 毕业生选择、2014 父母与子女、2019 选课
- 寓意：反映社会问题

### 1.3 教育类

- 主题：教育现象（教育方式、师生关系、学习态度）
- 例子：2018 选课（逆向选择）
- 寓意：分析教育问题

### 1.4 科技类

- 主题：科技发展（互联网、AI、社交）
- 例子：2015 手机时代、2021 运动健身
- 寓意：科技与生活

### 1.5 文化类

- 主题：文化交流（传统文化、跨文化、汉语热）
- 例子：2010 火锅、2020 汉语学习
- 寓意：文化交流

## 🟢 二、三段式黄金模板

### 2.1 第一段：描述图（2-3 句）

#### 模板 A：单图

> As is vividly depicted in the picture, [图描述]. The drawing aims at revealing a profound truth: [寓意].

#### 模板 B：双图对比

> As is vividly depicted in the picture, on the left is [图1], while on the right is [图2]. The contrast between the two pictures reveals [寓意].

#### 模板 C：图表

> As is shown in the chart, [图表描述]. The data indicates that [寓意].

#### 关键句式

- As is vividly depicted in the picture, ...（如图所示）
- The picture presents a thought-provoking scene: ...（图片呈现了发人深省的场景）
- In the cartoon, we can see ...（在漫画中，我们可以看到）
- The drawing reveals a common phenomenon: ...（图片揭示了一个普遍现象）
- The picture conveys a profound message: ...（图片传达了一个深刻的信息）

### 2.2 第二段：解释寓意（5-7 句）

#### 模板

> The picture conveys a profound meaning. [解释含义1], [解释含义2]. This phenomenon can be attributed to the following factors.
>
> First and foremost, [原因1]. For example, [举例1].
>
> Moreover, [原因2]. As the old saying goes, "[谚语]".
>
> Last but not least, [原因3]. In contemporary society, [举例2].

#### 关键句式

- The reasons can be listed as follows.
- There are several factors accounting for this phenomenon.
- First and foremost, ...; Moreover, ...; Last but not least, ...
- A case in point is that ...
- As is known to all, ...

### 2.3 第三段：评论 + 建议（4-6 句）

#### 模板

> From my perspective, [我的观点]. On the one hand, [建议1]. On the other hand, [建议2]. Only in this way can we [展望].
>
> As the proverb says, "[谚语]". I firmly believe that a bright future is awaiting us if we make concerted efforts.

#### 关键句式

- From my perspective, ...（在我看来）
- As far as I am concerned, ...（就我而言）
- Personally, I believe that ...（我个人认为）
- It is high time that we ...（是我们该…的时候了）
- Only in this way can we ...（只有这样我们才能…）
- I firmly believe that ...（我坚信）
- As the proverb says, ...（正如谚语所说）

## 🟡 三、10 大主题范文（占位，用户补充）

### 3.1 哲理类范文

#### 范文 1：2010 文化建设（文化火锅）

> As is vividly depicted in the picture, a hot pot contains many different ingredients, including Chinese and Western cultures, traditional and modern cultures, and so on. The drawing aims at revealing a profound truth: the integration of diverse cultures is the trend of our time.
>
> The picture conveys a profound meaning. Cultural integration promotes mutual understanding, economic development, and social progress. This phenomenon can be attributed to the following factors. First and foremost, with the development of globalization, different cultures have more opportunities to communicate. For example, Chinese culture has been spreading worldwide through Confucius Institutes. Moreover, the Internet has accelerated cultural exchange. Last but not least, more and more people are open to foreign cultures.
>
> From my perspective, cultural integration is essential for a country's development. On the one hand, we should preserve our own culture. On the other hand, we should learn from other cultures. Only in this way can we achieve cultural prosperity. As the proverb says, "Variety is the spice of life."

#### 范文 2：2011 旅程与环境

> As is vividly depicted in the picture, two people are traveling in a scenic spot, but they leave a lot of garbage behind. The drawing reveals a thought-provoking phenomenon: environmental pollution caused by tourism.
>
> The picture conveys a profound meaning. Environmental pollution not only damages the natural scenery but also threatens our health. This phenomenon can be attributed to the following factors. First and foremost, some tourists lack environmental awareness. For example, they throw garbage randomly in scenic spots. Moreover, the management of scenic spots is insufficient. Last but not least, the punishment for environmental damage is not severe enough.
>
> From my perspective, it is high time that we took action to protect the environment. On the one hand, the government should strengthen environmental regulations. On the other hand, the public should raise environmental awareness. Only in this way can we enjoy beautiful scenery. As the proverb says, "The earth is our common home."

## 🛠️ 四、5 大高级句式

### 4.1 倒装

- Only in this way can we achieve success.
- Not only does education shape our future, but also it shapes our character.
- Hard as he worked, he still failed.

### 4.2 强调

- It is **persistence** that leads to success.
- It was **the Internet** that changed our lives.

### 4.3 非谓语

- **Facing** the challenge, he didn't give up.
- **Encouraged** by his teacher, he worked harder.
- **To** achieve success, we must work hard.
- **Having finished** his homework, he went to bed.

### 4.4 虚拟

- **Were** I you, I would take action immediately.
- **If** I had studied harder, I would have succeeded.

### 4.5 It 句型

- It is universally acknowledged that ...
- It is high time that we took action.
- It is no doubt that ...
- It is a common phenomenon that ...
