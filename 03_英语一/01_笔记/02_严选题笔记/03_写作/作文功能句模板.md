# 作文功能句模板

> 本模板提供写作常用功能句，配合 [[03_英语一/01_笔记/02_严选题笔记/03_写作/写作模板]] 和 [[03_英语一/01_笔记/02_严选题笔记/02_语法/99_综合笔记_核心语法]] 使用

# 考研英语作文功能句模板（30个）

> 整理自24年考研实战笔记，经过真题验证

> - 每个功能句均可直接套用于小作文或大作文
> - 重点词汇用 <font color='red'>红色</font> 标注，需重点记忆
> - 语法要点用 <font color='brown'>棕色</font> 标注
> - 建议背诵后结合真题练习，做到灵活运用

---

## 一、开头段功能句（功能句1-3）

### 功能句1：自我介绍

**英文**：<font color='red'>As a senior at this university</font> who is <font color='red'>keen on</font> reading in this library in my spare time, I am writing for the purpose of making some suggestions to enhance the service here.

**中文**：作为一名喜欢在图书馆看书的本校大四学生，我写信来是为了提出一些建议来改进这里的服务。

**适用场景**：建议信、求助信开头，写给陌生人或机构时使用

**重点词汇**：
- <font color='red'>keen on</font>：热衷于、喜爱
- <font color='red'>for the purpose of</font>：为了……的目的
- <font color='red'>enhance</font>：提高、改进

**变体句**：I am Li Ming, an international student from China, <font color='red'>who</font> will study in your country.（我来自中国的留学生李明，将在贵国学习）

> 自我介绍 = 身份 + 定语从句（使写信人与收信人产生联系）

---

### 功能句2：寒暄

**英文**：I am <font color='red'>particularly delighted</font> to <font color='red'>recall</font> the past two months when I worked as an editor for your magazine.

**中文**：我很高兴回想起在贵杂志担任编辑的这两个月。

**适用场景**：辞职信、道歉信开头，写给熟人时使用

**重点词汇**：
- <font color='red'>particularly delighted</font>：特别高兴
- <font color='red'>recall</font>：回忆、回想

**变体句**：I am particularly delighted to recall last year when our class held the first charity sale.（非常高兴回想起去年我们班成功举办的首次慈善义卖活动）

> 寒暄句用于写给熟人的场景，首句寒暄，二句写目的

---

### 功能句3：目的（写信/通知）

**英文**：I am writing for the purpose of making suggestions for improving the service here.

**中文**：我写信来是想为改进这里的服务提一些建议。

**适用场景**：几乎所有小作文的第二句，用于引出写信目的

**重点词汇**：
- <font color='red'>for the purpose of</font>：为了……目的
- <font color='red'>recruiting</font>：招募

**变体句**：
1. This notice is for the purpose of <font color='red'>recruiting</font> 25 volunteers.（本通知旨在招募25名志愿者）
2. People tend to purchase presents to visit relatives and friends for the purpose of expressing their wishes.（人们通常会购买礼物走亲访友，以表达祝福）
3. This drawing/chart is for the purpose of illustrating…（图画/图表的主旨是说明……）

---

## 二、中间段功能句——建议与表达（功能句4-8）

### 功能句4：建议/要求

**英文**：<font color='red'>I deem that it is advisable for you to</font> apply for the position of English teaching in training organizations.

**中文**：我建议你申请培训机构的英语教学岗位。

**适用场景**：建议信中间段、提出具体建议时使用

**重点词汇**：
- <font color='red'>deem</font>：认为（=think）
- <font color='red'>advisable</font>：明智的、可取的
- <font color='red'>it is advisable for sb to do sth</font>：建议某人做某事

**变体句**：
1. You are <font color='red'>supposed to</font> prolong the opening hours of the reading <font color='red'>rooms where students study</font>.（你们应该延长阅览室的开放时间）
2. <font color='red'>Furthermore, it is advisable for parents to</font> give their kids freedom and opportunities to grow independently.（而且，家长应该给孩子提供独立成长的自由和机会）
3. Meanwhile, <font color='red'>it is of great necessity for them to</font> work out comprehensive strategies to enhance the quality of cars.（同时，他们有必要制定综合性的策略来提升汽车的质量）

> - you should = it is advisable for… to…
> - you should = it is of great necessity for… to…
> - you should = be supposed to

---

### 功能句5：展望未来

**英文**：If they do their utmost to do so, the future of the mobile phone industry will be <font color='red'>promising and hopeful</font>.

**中文**：如果他们竭尽全力，手机产业的未来将充满希望。

**适用场景**：大作文结尾段、展望积极前景时使用

**重点词汇**：
- <font color='red'>do one's utmost</font>：尽全力
- <font color='red'>promising</font>：有前途的、有希望的

---

### 功能句6：表达感谢或不满

**英文**：I want to say that I am <font color='red'>deeply grateful</font> to you for your hard work.

**中文**：我想说，非常感谢你们的辛勤工作。

**适用场景**：感谢信、投诉信中表达情感

**重点词汇**：
- <font color='red'>grateful/obliged/indebted/thankful to sb for sth</font>：因某事对某人感激

**不满变体句**：I am very disappointed with the <font color='red'>inferior</font> quality of your products.（我对你们的产品质量感到非常失望）

---

### 功能句7：表达期待

**英文**：I am <font color='red'>looking forward to</font> your favorable reply <font color='red'>at your earliest convenience</font>.

**中文**：我期待您的尽早肯定答复。

**适用场景**：书信结尾段，几乎所有小作文通用

**重点词汇**：
- <font color='red'>looking forward to</font>：期待
- <font color='red'>at your earliest convenience</font>：在您方便时尽早
- <font color='red'>favorable</font>：肯定的、赞同的

**变体句**：I sincerely hope that you can participate in this contest.（我真诚希望您能参加这次比赛）

---

### 功能句8：值得去做/关注

**英文**：This movie is <font color='red'>particularly worth watching</font> for two reasons.

**中文**：这部电影非常值得一看，有两个原因。

**适用场景**：推荐信、议论段中引出理由

**重点词汇**：
- <font color='red'>particularly worth doing</font>：特别值得做某事

**变体句**：These two pictures are particularly worth <font color='red'>attention</font> because they focus on how parents should educate their children in life and study.（这两幅图特别值得关注，因为它们聚焦的是父母在生活和学习方面应该如何教育孩子）

---

## 三、中间段功能句——原因与论证（功能句9-14）

### 功能句9：引出原因

**英文**：What resulted in the difference above? <font color='red'>From my perspective</font>, at least two reasons can <font color='red'>account for</font> this phenomenon.

**中文**：是什么导致了以上差异？在我看来，至少有两个原因可以解释这种现象。

**适用场景**：大作文中间段，引出分析原因

**重点词汇**：
- <font color='red'>account for</font>：解释、说明
- <font color='red'>From my perspective</font>：在我看来

---

### 功能句10：原因/重大意义（强调句）

**英文**：It is your <font color='red'>tremendous</font> work that <font color='red'>enabled</font> us to enjoy a positive atmosphere here.

**中文**：正是你们尽心尽力的工作使得我们在这里享受到了良好的学习氛围。

**适用场景**：强调原因、表达感谢、增强语气

**重点词汇**：
- <font color='red'>It is/was sth that enabled sb to do sth</font>：正是……使某人能够做某事
- <font color='red'>tremendous</font>：巨大的、极大的

**变体句**：It is their passive attitude that causes them not to see hope, chance, or possibility of change.（正是他们的悲观心态导致他们看不到希望、机会和改变的可能）

> It is/was + 被强调部分 + that + 其余部分

---

### 功能句11：原因（只关注……，忽视了……）

**英文**：This trend is particularly worth attention for the reason that they merely care about <u>the virtual world</u>, <font color='red'>ignoring</font> <u>the real life</u>.

**中文**：这种趋势很值得关注，因为这些人只关心虚拟世界，却忽视了现实生活。

**适用场景**：大作文中间段分析问题原因

**重点词汇**：
- <font color='red'>for the reason that</font>：因为（引导原因状语从句）
- <font color='red'>merely</font>：仅仅
- <font color='red'>ignoring</font>：忽视（现在分词表伴随）

---

### 功能句12：典型特征（+结果）

**英文**：College life <font color='red'>is characterized by</font> a rich variety of activities.

**中文**：大学生活的典型特征在于丰富多彩的活动。

**适用场景**：描述事物特征、图画联系现实

**重点词汇**：
- <font color='red'>be characterized by</font>：以……为特征
- <font color='red'>be marked by / be featured with</font>：同义替换

**变体句**：
1. Successful individuals are characterized by their various <font color='red'>virtues</font>, including <font color='red'>diligence, optimism, confidence, a strong will, and perseverance</font>.（成功人士的典型特征在于他们身上的各种优秀品质，包括勤奋、乐观、自信、意志坚强以及持之以恒）
2. In recent years, developing countries were characterized by their <font color='red'>economic boom</font>, which enabled local residents to possess additional money to purchase mobile phones.（近年来发展中国家的特征是经济蓬勃发展）

---

### 功能句13：对比

**英文**：<font color='red'>Compared with</font> the students' demands in study, the variety and quantity of books are not <font color='red'>adequate</font>.

**中文**：与学生们的需求相比，书的种类和数量是不足的。

**适用场景**：图表描述、比较分析

**重点词汇**：
- <font color='red'>Compared with</font>：与……相比
- <font color='red'>adequate</font>：充足的、足够的

**变体句**：Compared with the past years, the relations between China and Japan became worse, so <font color='red'>a host of</font> residents in China did not purchase Japanese products.（与过去相比，中日关系变差，因此很多中国居民不再购买日本产品）

---

### 功能句14：积极意义（排比）

**英文**：Practice and <font color='red'>diligence</font> will <font color='red'>bring</font> you inspiration, <font color='red'>arouse</font> your passion for study, and <font color='red'>help</font> you to make progress every day.

**中文**：实践和勤奋会带给你灵感，唤起你对学习的热情，并帮助你每天进步。

**适用场景**：大作文中间段论述积极意义

**重点词汇**：
- <font color='red'>bring sb sth, arouse sb's passion for sth, and help sb to do sth</font>：排比三连结构

**变体句**：
1. I deem that perseverance can always bring me power, arouse my passion for the future and help me to overcome so many hardships.（持之以恒的精神总能带给我力量，唤起我对未来的热情，帮助我克服困难）
2. Studying abroad can bring young people more opportunities, arouse their enthusiasm for foreign cultures, and help them to <font color='red'>broaden their horizons</font>.（出国留学带给年轻人更多机会，唤起他们对外国文化的热情，帮助他们开阔视野）

> … can **bring** sb + 名词, **arouse** sb's + 名词, and **help** sb to do sth.
> 此排比结构适用于任何积极意义的论述，仅替换主语和名词即可。

---

## 四、中间段功能句——事件与描写（功能句15-19）

### 功能句15：事件背景

**英文**：The ceremony <font color='red'>is scheduled to</font> start at 9:00 am on June 26th in the Center for Students' Activities, which will <font color='red'>last for</font> about two hours.

**中文**：典礼定于6月26日上午9:00在学生活动中心开始，将持续约2小时。

**适用场景**：通知、邀请信中说明活动时间和地点

**重点词汇**：
- <font color='red'>be scheduled to</font>：定于、计划于
- <font color='red'>last for</font>：持续

**变体句**：The international conference on globalization is scheduled to be held in our university.（有关全球化的国际会议将在我校举办）

---

### 功能句16：说明忙碌

**英文**：As you know, during the past half year, I was <font color='red'>extremely busy</font> because I focused all my attention on the preparation for <font color='red'>the national entrance examination for postgraduate studies</font>.

**中文**：您是知道的，在过去的这半年中，我非常忙碌，因为我把所有注意力都放在研究生入学考试的准备上。

**适用场景**：道歉信、辞职信中解释原因

**重点词汇**：
- <font color='red'>the national entrance examination for postgraduate studies</font>：研究生入学考试
- <font color='red'>focus all my attention on</font>：集中所有注意力于

---

### 功能句17：个人观点

**英文**：<font color='red'>As a senior, who is preparing for</font> the national entrance exams for postgraduate studies, I am <font color='red'>firmly convinced</font> that the spirit of perseverance can always bring me power.

**中文**：作为一名正在准备研究生入学考试的大四学生，我坚信持之以恒的精神总会带给我力量。

**适用场景**：大作文结尾段表达个人观点

**重点词汇**：
- <font color='red'>be firmly convinced that</font>：坚信
- <font color='red'>perseverance</font>：持之以恒、毅力

**变体句**：I, <font color='red'>as a youngster</font>, prefer to work in the city and live in the countryside, for...（作为一名年轻人，我喜欢在城市工作、在乡村生活，因为……）

---

### 功能句18：概述图画/材料

**英文**：This is a simple but <font color='red'>enlightening</font> picture which <font color='red'>vividly illustrates</font> a common phenomenon among…

**中文**：这是一张简单但具有启发性的图画，生动展示了存在于……之中的一个普遍现象。

**适用场景**：大作文第一段开头描述图画

**重点词汇**：
- <font color='red'>enlightening</font>：有启发性的
- <font color='red'>vividly illustrates</font>：生动地展示了

**变体句**：This is a simple but enlightening excerpt of an article, in which the author points out that…（这是一篇简单但具有启发性的文章节选，作者在文中指出……）

> enlightening 可替换为：inspiring（鼓舞人心的）、ironic（讽刺的）、thought-provoking（发人深省的）

---

### 功能句19：图画细节描写（时间-地点-人/物-行为-伴随）

**英文**：On the path to a high mountain, there is a youngster, sitting for a rest and sighing, "I am too exhausted to climb."

**中文**：在通往高山的路上，有一个年轻人正坐着休息，并叹气道："累了！我不爬了。"

**适用场景**：大作文第一段描述图画具体内容

**重点词汇/句式**：
- <font color='brown'>全倒装：介词短语 + 谓语动词 + 主语</font>
- <font color='brown'>with + n. + doing/v-ed/介词短语：表身上或周围的事物</font>

**变体句**：
1. <font color='red'>Beside a table sit</font> four youngsters, staring at their smartphones, <font color='red'>with</font> delicious food <font color='red'>uneaten</font>.（四个年轻人坐在餐桌旁，盯着手机，美食丝毫未动）
2. <font color='red'>In front of a computer sits</font> a teacher, giving a lesson to students, <font color='red'>with</font> a sweet smile <font color='red'>on his face</font>.（一台电脑前坐着一位老师，正在给学生上课，脸上带着微笑）

> 1. 介词短语表环境
> 2. there be 引出人/物
> 3. v-ing 表伴随的动作
> 4. with + n. + v-ing/v-ed/介词短语 表身上或周围的事物
> 5. although 引导让步状语，表反差
> 6. 被动句 + 定语从句：文字说明

---

## 五、中间段功能句——对比与引出观点（功能句20-23）

### 功能句20：对比反差/引出观点

**英文**：<font color='red'>Although</font> they gather to enhance friendship, these people <font color='red'>scarcely</font> chat with each other.

**中文**：尽管他们是为了增进友谊而聚餐，但这些人彼此之间几乎没有交流。

**适用场景**：描述图画中的反差现象，引出讨论观点

**重点词汇**：
- <font color='red'>scarcely</font>：几乎不（否定副词）
- <font color='brown'>半倒装：表否定概念的副词或短语位于主语之前</font>

**变体句**：Although I do not have children, I <font color='red'>cannot agree more with</font> the author's idea.（尽管我没有孩子，但我完全同意作者的观点）

---

### 功能句21：文字说明

**英文**：Two Chinese characters below the picture can be noticed, which say "<font color='red'>on the way</font>."

**中文**：图画下面的两个汉字可以被注意到，写的是"途中"。

**适用场景**：大作文中引用图画上的文字

**重点词汇**：
- <font color='red'>can be noticed</font>：可以被注意到（被动语态）
- <font color='red'>which say</font>：写的是（定语从句）

---

### 功能句22：概述图表

**英文**：This is a <font color='red'>column chart</font>, which clearly illustrates the remarkable changes in the shares of Chinese, American and Japanese car brands in the Chinese market from 2008 to 2009.

**中文**：这是一个柱状图，清晰展示了2008年到2009年中、美、日系汽车品牌在中国汽车市场份额的明显变化。

**适用场景**：图表作文第一段描述图表

**重点词汇**：
- <font color='red'>column chart</font>：柱状图
- <font color='red'>remarkable</font>：显著的、明显的
- <font color='red'>shares</font>：份额

---

### 功能句23：数据上升/下降/稳定

**英文**：During the period, the share of Chinese brands increased <font color='red'>remarkably</font>, reaching about 33%. <font color='red'>By contrast</font>, the sales of Japanese brands displayed a <font color='red'>sharp decline</font>, from 35% to 26%. <font color='red'>Meanwhile</font>, the proportion of American brands <font color='red'>remained stable</font> at 12%.

**中文**：在此期间，中国品牌的份额明显上升，达到约33%。相比之下，日系品牌的销量呈现出急剧下降的趋势，从35%降至26%。同时，美系品牌的占比稳定在12%。

**适用场景**：图表作文描述数据变化趋势

**重点词汇**：
- <font color='red'>increased remarkably</font>：显著上升
- <font color='red'>sharp decline</font>：急剧下降
- <font color='red'>remained stable</font>：保持稳定
- <font color='red'>by contrast</font>：相比之下
- <font color='red'>meanwhile</font>：与此同时

---

## 六、中间段功能句——占比与联系主旨（功能句24-26）

### 功能句24：占比排序

**英文**：Overall, the expense on New Year gifts has the <font color='red'>largest proportion</font>, accounting for 40%. The next is the expense on transportation and dining, with the same 20%. <font color='red'>Finally comes</font> the total <font color='red'>spending on</font> others, with 20%.

**中文**：总体上，新年礼物的花销占最大比重，达到40%。其次是交通和聚餐费用，均占20%。最后是其他花销的总和，为20%。

**适用场景**：图表作文中描述占比和排序

**重点词汇**：
- <font color='red'>largest proportion</font>：最大比重
- <font color='red'>accounting for</font>：占比
- <font color='blue'>Finally comes + 主语</font>：全倒装句式

> Finally comes + 主语 — 用于排序描述中的最后一位

---

### 功能句25：图画/图表联系主旨

**英文**：This drawing/picture/cartoon, <font color='red'>apparently</font>, can be <font color='red'>associated with</font> two distinct attitudes in front of difficulties.

**中文**：显然，这幅图能让人联想到面对逆境时两种截然不同的态度。

**适用场景**：大作文中间段，从图画过渡到现实讨论

**重点词汇**：
- <font color='red'>apparently</font>：显然
- <font color='red'>be associated with</font>：与……联系起来
- <font color='red'>distinct</font>：截然不同的

**变体句**：Such data, apparently, can be associated with the fierce competition of the Chinese car market.（这样的数据能让人联想到中国汽车市场的激烈竞争）

---

### 功能句26：普遍现象

**英文**：<font color='red'>However, in reality</font>, <font color='red'>a host of</font> young people tend to give up when they encounter challenges during their pursuit of success.

**中文**：然而，在现实生活中，很多年轻人在追求成功的过程中，往往一遇到挑战便会放弃。

**适用场景**：大作文中间段，引出社会普遍现象

**重点词汇**：
- <font color='red'>a host of</font>：大量的（=a great number of）
- <font color='red'>tend to</font>：倾向于、往往
- <font color='red'>in reality</font>：在现实中

---

## 七、结尾段功能句（功能句27-30）

### 功能句27：危害

**英文**：Actually, the thought of "giving up" may form an <font color='red'>obstacle</font>, which <font color='red'>hinders</font> many people's growth and progress in work, life and study.

**中文**：事实上，"放弃"这种想法可能会构成一种障碍，阻碍着很多人在工作、生活和学习中取得进步和成长。

**适用场景**：大作文中间段论述危害

**重点词汇**：
- <font color='red'>obstacle</font>：障碍
- <font color='red'>hinder</font>：阻碍、妨碍
- <font color='red'>form an obstacle</font>：构成障碍

---

### 功能句28：观点+比喻

**英文**：<font color='red'>From my perspective</font>, confidence can <font color='red'>be likened to</font> a key, which unlocks the gate to a promising future.

**中文**：在我看来，自信可以被看做一把钥匙，打开通往光明未来的大门。

**适用场景**：大作文结尾段，用比喻升华主题

**重点词汇**：
- <font color='red'>be likened to</font>：被比作
- <font color='red'>unlock the gate to</font>：打开通往……的大门
- <font color='red'>promising</font>：光明的、有前途的

> **主语** can be likened to **喻体**, which **谓语 + 宾语**
> 例如：Traditional culture can be likened to a bridge, which links the past and today.（传统文化可以被比作一座桥梁，连接着过去和今天）

---

### 功能句29：号召

**英文**：Thus, everyone should <font color='red'>bear in mind</font> that "Never give up. You will eventually achieve your dream."

**中文**：因此，每个人都应该牢记：永不放弃，你的梦想终会实现。

**适用场景**：大作文结尾段，发出号召

**重点词汇**：
- <font color='red'>bear in mind</font>：牢记
- <font color='red'>Society as a whole</font>：整个社会
- <font color='red'>advocate/criticize</font>：倡导/批判

**变体句**：<font color='red'>Society as a whole</font> should foster a positive atmosphere to <font color='red'>advocate</font> public virtues.（整个社会应该营造一种积极氛围，以倡导公共美德）

> Society as a whole should foster a positive atmosphere to advocate/criticize + 某种行为/品质。

---

### 功能句30：举例/列举

**英文**：A typical example is me.

**中文**：一个典型例子就是我自己。

**适用场景**：举例论证、在其他功能句后进行详述

**重点词汇**：
- <font color='red'>technological</font>：科技的

**变体句**：We are supposed to utilize modern <font color='red'>technological</font> products, including the Internet, cell phones, TV and computers.（我们应该利用现代科技产品，包括网络、手机、电视以及电脑）

### 范文2：龙舟赛（大作文）

This is a simple but <font color='red'>enlightening</font> cartoon, in which there is a dragon-boat race, with <font color='red'>a host of</font> villagers standing around and cheering up. Meanwhile, an old person chatted with his wife "Wonderful! The dragon boat race in our village is becoming increasingly lively!"

China, as an ancient civilized country, is <font color='red'>characterized by</font> its profound history and various cultural traditions, such as the Mid-Autumn Festival, the Dragon Boat Festival and the Spring Festival.

In order to inherit the cultural heritage, Chinese people still hold a variety of traditional ceremonies and activities. This phenomenon is particularly worth attention <font color='red'>for the reason that</font> these activities can <font color='red'>bring</font> us a positive atmosphere, <font color='red'>arouse</font> our passion for cultural tradition, and <font color='red'>help</font> us to know our history better.

Thus, from my perspective, the traditional culture can be <font color='red'>likened to</font> a bridge, which links the past and today. In addition, everyone should <font color='red'>bear in mind</font> that "our history and culture is the root of our nation."
