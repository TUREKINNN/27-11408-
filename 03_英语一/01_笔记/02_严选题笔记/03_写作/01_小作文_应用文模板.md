# 📚 英语一 · 小作文应用文模板

> **建立时间**：2026-06-23
> **目标**：小作文 7-8 分（满分 10 分）
> **说明**：10 大类型书信 + 通知 + 备忘录

---

## 🎯 一、10 大书信类型

### 1.1 建议信

**适用场景**：对某事提出建议（学习、工作、生活）

**结构**：
- 第一段：自我介绍 + 写信目的
- 第二段：具体建议（3 条）
- 第三段：总结 + 期望

**模板**：
> Dear [称呼],
> I am [自我介绍]. I am writing to offer you some suggestions on [主题].
> To begin with, I suggest that you [建议1]. Moreover, it would be beneficial if you [建议2]. Last but not least, [建议3].
> I hope these suggestions will be of some help to you. I am looking forward to your good news.
> Yours sincerely,
> Li Ming

**常用词汇**：
- suggest, recommend, advise
- proposal, advice, suggestion
- beneficial, helpful, useful

### 1.2 感谢信

**适用场景**：对帮助、礼物、款待表示感谢

**结构**：
- 第一段：表达感谢
- 第二段：具体感谢的事
- 第三段：表达回报

**模板**：
> Dear [称呼],
> I am writing to express my sincere gratitude to you for [感谢的事].
> During [时间], you [具体帮助]. I was deeply moved by your [品质]. Thanks to your help, I was able to [结果].
> I would like to invite you to [回报方式] to show my appreciation. I am looking forward to seeing you soon.
> Yours sincerely,
> Li Ming

**常用词汇**：
- grateful, thankful, appreciate
- gratitude, thanks, appreciation
- kindness, hospitality, generosity

### 1.3 邀请信

**适用场景**：邀请参加活动（聚会、讲座、会议）

**结构**：
- 第一段：发出邀请
- 第二段：活动详情
- 第三段：期待回复

**模板**：
> Dear [称呼],
> I am writing to invite you to [活动].
> The [活动类型] will be held at [地点] on [时间]. The theme is [主题]. We will have [活动内容]. I believe you will enjoy it.
> I would be grateful if you could accept my invitation. I am looking forward to your reply.
> Yours sincerely,
> Li Ming

**常用词汇**：
- invite, invitation
- participate, attend, join
- event, activity, gathering

### 1.4 道歉信

**适用场景**：因某种原因表示歉意

**结构**：
- 第一段：表达歉意
- 第二段：解释原因
- 第三段：补救措施

**模板**：
> Dear [称呼],
> I am writing to make an apology to you for [道歉的事].
> I am truly sorry that I [具体行为]. The reason is that [原因]. I understand that this has caused you [后果].
> To make up for my mistake, I will [补救措施]. I sincerely hope that you can forgive me.
> Yours sincerely,
> Li Ming

**常用词汇**：
- apologize, apology, sorry
- regret, remorse
- forgive, excuse, pardon

### 1.5 推荐信

**适用场景**：推荐某人或某物

**结构**：
- 第一段：表明推荐意图
- 第二段：推荐对象的优点
- 第三段：总结推荐

**模板**：
> Dear [称呼],
> I am writing to recommend [被推荐人/物] for [目的].
> [被推荐人/物] is [被推荐对象的身份]. I have known him/her/it for [时间]. I was deeply impressed by his/her/its [优点1]. Moreover, [优点2]. In addition, [优点3].
> I firmly believe that [被推荐人/物] is an ideal choice. I would appreciate it if you could give him/her/it a chance.
> Yours sincerely,
> Li Ming

**常用词汇**：
- recommend, recommendation
- outstanding, excellent, remarkable
- ideal, perfect, suitable

### 1.6 求职信

**适用场景**：申请工作

**结构**：
- 第一段：表明求职意图
- 第二段：个人优势
- 第三段：期待面试

**模板**：
> Dear [称呼],
> I am writing to apply for the position of [职位] advertised in [来源].
> I graduated from [学校] with a degree in [专业]. I have [经验1] and [经验2]. I am proficient in [技能]. I am confident that I am qualified for the position.
> I would be grateful if you could grant me an interview. I am looking forward to your reply.
> Yours sincerely,
> Li Ming

**常用词汇**：
- apply, application
- qualified, experienced, skilled
- position, vacancy, post

### 1.7 辞职信

**适用场景**：辞职

**结构**：
- 第一段：表明辞职意图
- 第二段：辞职原因
- 第三段：表达感谢

**模板**：
> Dear [称呼],
> I am writing to inform you of my decision to resign from [职位].
> The reason is that [原因]. I have learned a lot during my [时间] at [公司]. I am grateful for the opportunities and experience you have given me.
> I will do my best to ensure a smooth transition. I wish you and [公司] continued success.
> Yours sincerely,
> Li Ming

**常用词汇**：
- resign, resignation
- decision, intention
- grateful, appreciate, acknowledge

### 1.8 投诉信

**适用场景**：对某事表达不满

**结构**：
- 第一段：表明投诉意图
- 第二段：投诉原因
- 第三段：要求解决

**模板**：
> Dear [称呼],
> I am writing to complain about [投诉的事].
> I purchased [产品/服务] from you on [日期]. However, [问题]. I have tried [已尝试的解决方式], but the problem remains.
> I would appreciate it if you could [要求]. I expect [期望的解决方案]. Please give me a reply at your earliest convenience.
> Yours sincerely,
> Li Ming

**常用词汇**：
- complain, complaint
- dissatisfied, disappointed, unsatisfied
- refund, replace, compensate

### 1.9 祝贺信

**适用场景**：祝贺对方取得成就

**结构**：
- 第一段：表达祝贺
- 第二段：评价成就
- 第三段：表达祝愿

**模板**：
> Dear [称呼],
> I am writing to congratulate you on [成就].
> I learned that you [具体成就]. This is a great achievement, and you deserve it. I have always known that you are [评价].
> I wish you continued success in the future. May all your dreams come true.
> Yours sincerely,
> Li Ming

**常用词汇**：
- congratulate, congratulations
- achievement, success, accomplishment
- deserve, earn, merit

### 1.10 慰问信

**适用场景**：慰问生病、遭遇困难的人

**结构**：
- 第一段：表达关心
- 第二段：表示支持
- 第三段：表达祝愿

**模板**：
> Dear [称呼],
> I am writing to express my concern about [情况].
> I am sorry to hear that [具体问题]. I want you to know that I am thinking of you. I believe you will [鼓励].
> I hope you will [祝愿]. Please let me know if there is anything I can do for you.
> Yours sincerely,
> Li Ming

**常用词汇**：
- concern, sympathy, care
- support, comfort, encourage
- recover, get well, overcome

## 🟢 二、通知（Notice）

**结构**：
- 标题
- 时间、地点、事件
- 参加对象
- 联系方式

**模板**：
> **Notice**
>
> In order to [目的], [组织] is organizing a [活动] for [对象]. The [活动] will be held at [地点] on [时间]. The theme is [主题]. Activities will include [内容].
>
> All [对象] are welcome to participate. For further information, please contact [联系方式].
>
> The Student Union
> June 23, 2026

## 🟡 三、备忘录（Memo）

**结构**：
- To, From, Date, Subject
- 内容

**模板**：
> **Memo**
>
> To: [接收人]
> From: [发送人]
> Date: [日期]
> Subject: [主题]
>
> I am writing to [目的]. [具体内容]. I would appreciate it if you could [要求].
>
> Thank you for your attention.

## 🛠️ 四、10 大高级替换词

| 低级词 | 高级词 | 例句 |
|:------|:-------|:-----|
| important | vital, crucial, essential | This is **vital** to our success. |
| very | extremely, highly, remarkably | He is **extremely** smart. |
| good | excellent, outstanding, superior | This is an **excellent** book. |
| bad | terrible, awful, inferior | The food was **terrible**. |
| many | numerous, countless, a wealth of | There are **numerous** reasons. |
| big | enormous, massive, considerable | This is an **enormous** task. |
| more and more | increasingly, growingly | The problem is **increasingly** serious. |
| think | believe, hold, claim, maintain | I **maintain** that... |
| help | assist, facilitate, contribute to | This will **facilitate** your work. |
| show | demonstrate, indicate, reveal, illustrate | This **illustrates** the point. |

## 🛠️ 五、5 个亮点句式

### 5.1 谚语/警句

- As the old saying goes, "Practice makes perfect."
- There is no smoke without fire.
- Where there is a will, there is a way.
- Actions speak louder than words.
- A friend in need is a friend indeed.

### 5.2 名人事例

- 例子 1：马云 (Jack Ma) 创立阿里巴巴，改变了中国的电商
- 例子 2：屠呦呦 (Tu Youyou) 获得诺贝尔医学奖
- 例子 3：袁隆平 (Yuan Longping) 解决了中国粮食问题
- 例子 4：钟南山 (Zhong Nanshan) 在新冠疫情中的贡献
- 例子 5：姚明 (Yao Ming) 推动了中美篮球交流

### 5.3 高级句式

#### 倒装

- Only in this way can we solve the problem.
- Not only does he speak English, but also he speaks French.

#### 强调

- It is **persistence** that leads to success.
- It was **hard work** that made him successful.

#### 非谓语

- **Believing** in himself, he overcame all difficulties.
- **Encouraged** by his teacher, he worked harder.

#### 虚拟

- **Were** I you, I would take action immediately.
- **If** I had studied harder, I would have succeeded.

#### It 句型

- It is universally acknowledged that...
- It is high time that we took action.
