## 🎯 范文 1：建议信

> **题目**：你是李明，你的英国朋友 Peter 想学中文，请给他一些建议。

**范文**：

Dear Peter,

I am Li Ming, a Chinese student who is studying in the same university as you. I am writing to offer you some suggestions on learning Chinese.

To begin with, I suggest that you learn pinyin first, which is the foundation of Chinese pronunciation. Moreover, it would be beneficial if you practice speaking Chinese with native speakers, which can help you improve your oral skills. Last but not least, watching Chinese movies and listening to Chinese songs can help you immerse yourself in the language.

I hope these suggestions will be of some help to you. I am looking forward to hearing your progress in Chinese.

Yours sincerely,
Li Ming

## 🎯 范文 2：感谢信

> **题目**：你是李明，Tom 帮你找到了失物，写信感谢他。

**范文**：

Dear Tom,

I am writing to express my sincere gratitude to you for helping me find my lost wallet.

Last Sunday, I lost my wallet on the playground. I was very worried because there was my ID card and some cash in it. You spent two hours looking for it and finally found it near the library. I was deeply moved by your kindness and helpfulness. Thanks to your help, I got back my wallet and all the important things.

I would like to invite you to dinner this Saturday to show my appreciation. I am looking forward to seeing you soon.

Yours sincerely,
Li Ming

## 🎯 范文 4：道歉信

> **题目**：你答应朋友参加聚会，但因生病无法参加，写信道歉。

**范文**：

Dear John,

I am writing to make an apology to you for not being able to attend your birthday party.

I was really looking forward to your party, but unfortunately, I caught a severe flu yesterday. The doctor advised me to stay in bed for a few days. I understand that my absence will affect the atmosphere of the party, and I feel very sorry about that.

To make up for my absence, I will send you a birthday gift and visit you next week when I recover. I sincerely hope that you can understand my situation.

Yours sincerely,
Li Ming

**范文**：

I have enjoyed working with my colleagues, who are so professional and friendly.

I will do my best to ensure a smooth transition of my work. I will complete all my current projects and hand over my work to my colleagues before I leave. I wish you and the company continued success.

Yours sincerely,
Li Ming

I contacted your customer service immediately, but they refused to refund or replace it, saying that the damage was caused by me. I have tried emailing and calling them, but the problem remains.

I would appreciate it if you could refund the full amount of $300 or send me a new camera. I expect to hear from you within seven days. I trust you will give my complaint your immediate attention.

Yours sincerely,
Li Ming
