# 英语一考研写作模板

## 一、应用文模板

### 1. 建议信 (Suggestion Letter)

```
Dear [Sir/Madam / Mr. Wang],

I am [自我介绍], writing to express my suggestions concerning [建议的事由].

Here are my proposals for your reference. First and foremost, I would like to suggest that [建议一], which would [好处]. In addition, it would be advisable if you could [建议二], as this may help [好处]. Last but not least, I strongly recommend that [建议三], enabling [好处].

I sincerely hope that you will find these suggestions helpful. I would be more than happy to discuss this matter further.

Yours sincerely,
Li Ming
```

### 2. 邀请信 (Invitation Letter)

```
Dear [Professor Smith],

I am [自我介绍]. On behalf of [组织], I am writing to cordially invite you to [活动/事件].

The event is scheduled to take place on [日期] at [地点], which will involve [活动内容简述]. As [原因/您的声望], your participation would be of great significance. You are kindly invited to [具体邀请内容，如deliver a speech on...].

We would cover all expenses related to your travel and accommodation. Should you accept this invitation, please let us know at your earliest convenience.

We are looking forward to your favorable reply.

Yours sincerely,
Li Ming
```

### 3. 感谢信 (Thank-you Letter)

```
Dear [Mr. Wang],

I am writing to extend my sincere gratitude to you for [感谢的具体事项].

[具体描述对方的帮助]. It was so kind of you to [对方所做的事情]. Had it not been for your generous help, I would not have been able to [结果]. Your [品质，如warmth/kindness/patience] left a deep impression on me.

I hope I may have the opportunity of returning the favor someday. Please accept my heartfelt thanks again.

Yours sincerely,
Li Ming
```

### 4. 投诉信 (Complaint Letter)

```
Dear [Sir/Madam],

I am [自我介绍]. I am writing to file a complaint about [产品/服务].

I purchased/ordered [商品] on [日期] from [地点]. To my great disappointment, [具体问题描述]. Not only did this cause [损失一], but it also resulted in [损失二].

I sincerely hope that you will look into this matter and give me a satisfactory reply. I would appreciate it if you could [解决方案：退款/更换/道歉]. If the problem is not resolved by [时间], I will have to take further action.

Yours sincerely,
Li Ming
```

### 5. 申请信 (Application Letter)

```
Dear [Sir/Madam],

I am [自我介绍, 如a senior student from Peking University]. I am writing to apply for [申请的职位/机会].

I believe I am well qualified for this position. To begin with, [优势一：专业/学历]. Furthermore, [优势二：经验/技能]. Last but not least, [优势三：个人品质]. All these make me confident that I can [胜任的具体工作].

I have enclosed my resume for your review. I would be grateful if you could take my application into consideration. I am available for an interview at your convenience.

Yours sincerely,
Li Ming
```

## 二、通知与备忘录格式

### 1. 通知 (Notice)

```
                        NOTICE

                   [通知标题，如 English Speech Contest]

January 15, 2025

[正文第一段：说明活动背景/目的]
In order to enrich students' campus life and improve their English proficiency, the Students' Union has decided to organize an English Speech Contest.

[正文第二段：活动具体信息]
The contest is scheduled to be held in the Lecture Hall on January 25, 2025. All students are welcome to participate. Those who are interested are required to sign up at Room 301, Building 5, before January 20.

[正文第三段：联系方式/其他信息]
For further information, please contact Li Ming at 123-4567-8900 or send an email to liming@email.com.

                                    Students' Union
```

### 2. 备忘录 (Memo)

```
                        MEMO

To:   All Staff
From: Li Ming, General Manager
Date: January 15, 2025
Subject: Annual Meeting Arrangement

[正文]
I am writing to inform you of the annual meeting arrangements.

The annual meeting will be held on January 25, 2025 (Friday) from 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM in the Conference Room on the 5th floor. All staff members are required to attend.

Please prepare a brief summary of your work over the past year and submit it to your department head before January 20.

Your cooperation would be greatly appreciated.
```

## 三、短文写作（图画作文模板）

### 整体框架：三段式

### 1. 描述段 (第一段)

```
As is vividly depicted in the picture/cartoon, [描述图画中的主体和动作].
What is striking/interesting is that [图画中的突出细节].
Obviously, the picture reveals a thought-provoking phenomenon: [点明图画寓意].
```

**变体句式：**
- `The picture above presents us with a scene in which...`
- `As is symbolically illustrated in the cartoon, ...`
- `In the picture, there is/are... , which indicates that...`

### 2. 论述段 (第二段)

```
The intended meaning of the picture is both profound and thought-provoking. There are several reasons accounting for this phenomenon. To begin with, [原因一]. Furthermore, [原因二]. Last but not least, [原因三].

A case in point is [举例论证]. This example clearly demonstrates that [总结].
```

**变体句式：**
- `Several factors may contribute to this phenomenon. First and foremost, ... In addition, ...`
- `The picture is designed to draw our attention to... , which has become a growing concern in contemporary society.`

### 3. 总结段 (第三段)

```
In my view / Taking all these factors into consideration, effective measures should be taken to [解决问题]. On the one hand, it is imperative for [主体一] to [措施一]. On the other hand, [主体二] should [措施二]. Only in this way can we [美好愿景].
```

**变体句式：**
- `In conclusion, ... is of great significance to both individuals and society as a whole.`
- `It is high time that we took concrete actions to...`

## 四、10个高分句型

### 1. 倒装句 — Not only...but also...
> **Not only does** reading broaden our horizons, **but it also** enriches our spiritual life.

用法：Not only置于句首时，前半句倒装（助动词提前）。

### 2. 形式主语 — It is universally acknowledged that...
> **It is universally acknowledged that** education plays a pivotal role in the development of an individual.

替代：It is widely accepted / generally recognized / commonly believed that...

### 3. 虚拟语气 — It is high time that...
> **It is high time that** we **took** effective measures to protect the environment.

用法：从句用过去时或should+动词原形。

### 4. 强调句 — It is...that...
> **It is** through persistent effort **that** one can achieve remarkable success.

结构：It is/was + 被强调部分 + that/who + 其余

### 5. only倒装 — Only in this way can...
> **Only by** raising public awareness **can** we solve this problem once and for all.

用法：Only + 状语置于句首，主句部分倒装。

### 6. 让步状语 — No matter how...
> **No matter how** difficult the challenge may be, we should never give up.

同义替换：However difficult the challenge may be, ...

### 7. 存在句变体 — There is no denying that...
> **There is no denying that** technology has transformed every aspect of our lives.

同义：There is no doubt that / It cannot be denied that

### 8. with复合结构
> **With** the rapid development of economy, people's living standards have improved significantly.

也用于伴随状况：He stood there, **with his eyes fixed on** the screen.

### 9. 定语从句嵌套 — ..., which, in turn, ...
> An increasing number of young people are addicted to smartphones, **which**, in turn, **leads to** a decline in their academic performance.

### 10. so...that... 结果状语
> **So** compelling **is** the evidence **that** even the most skeptical critics are convinced.

倒装形式（So+adj.置于句首）更加分。

## 五、写作万能衔接词

| 功能 | 词汇/短语 |
|------|----------|
| 递进 | furthermore, moreover, in addition, besides, what's more |
| 转折 | however, nevertheless, on the contrary, conversely |
| 因果 | therefore, consequently, as a result, hence |
| 举例 | for instance, for example, a case in point is |
| 总结 | in conclusion, to sum up, in a word, all in all |
| 对比 | in contrast, on the other hand, while, whereas |

## 六、写作注意事项

1. **字数**：小作文约100-120词，大作文约160-200词
2. **格式**：应用文注意称呼、落款的格式规范
3. **卷面**：书写工整，不要涂改
4. **模板使用**：模板要灵活运用，切忌生搬硬套
5. **高级词汇替换**：important → pivotal/vital/crucial；think → maintain/contend/reckon
