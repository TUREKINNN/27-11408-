## 🎯 范文 1：2010 文化建设（文化火锅）

> **题目**：根据图片描述，写一篇 160-200 词的文章。

**范文**：

As is vividly depicted in the picture, a hot pot contains many different ingredients, including Chinese and Western cultures, traditional and modern cultures, and so on. The drawing aims at revealing a profound truth: the integration of diverse cultures is the trend of our time.

The picture conveys a profound meaning. Cultural integration promotes mutual understanding, economic development, and social progress. This phenomenon can be attributed to the following factors. First and foremost, with the development of globalization, different cultures have more opportunities to communicate. For example, Chinese culture has been spreading worldwide through Confucius Institutes. Moreover, the Internet has accelerated cultural exchange, making it easier for people to learn about foreign cultures. Last but not least, more and more people are open to foreign cultures, which has greatly enriched their lives.

From my perspective, cultural integration is essential for a country's development. On the one hand, we should preserve our own cultural heritage. On the other hand, we should learn from other cultures. Only in this way can we achieve cultural prosperity. As the proverb says, "Variety is the spice of life."

## 🎯 范文 2：2011 旅程与环境

> **题目**：根据图片描述，写一篇 160-200 词的文章。

**范文**：

As is vividly depicted in the picture, two people are traveling in a scenic spot, but they leave a lot of garbage behind. The drawing reveals a thought-provoking phenomenon: environmental pollution caused by tourism.

The picture conveys a profound meaning. Environmental pollution not only damages the natural scenery but also threatens our health and the ecosystem. This phenomenon can be attributed to the following factors. First and foremost, some tourists lack environmental awareness, and they throw garbage randomly in scenic spots. Moreover, the management of scenic spots is insufficient; there are not enough garbage bins and signs. Last but not least, the punishment for environmental damage is not severe enough to deter such behavior.

From my perspective, it is high time that we took action to protect the environment. On the one hand, the government should strengthen environmental regulations and increase penalties. On the other hand, the public should raise environmental awareness and develop eco-friendly travel habits. Only in this way can we enjoy beautiful scenery without harming the environment. As the proverb says, "The earth is our common home."

## 🎯 范文 3：2012 乐观与悲观

**范文**：

As is vividly depicted in the picture, two people are faced with the same half-filled bottle of water. One is smiling, saying "It's half full", while the other is frowning, saying "It's half empty". The contrast reveals a thought-provoking truth: different attitudes lead to different outcomes.

The picture conveys a profound meaning. Optimism and pessimism represent two different ways of looking at life. Optimists focus on what they have, while pessimists focus on what they lack. This difference in attitude can be attributed to the following factors. First and foremost, life experiences shape our attitudes. For example, people who have overcome difficulties tend to be more optimistic. Moreover, social support plays an important role. Last but not least, personal character matters.

From my perspective, an optimistic attitude is essential for success and happiness. On the one hand, optimism helps us overcome challenges. On the other hand, pessimism can lead to anxiety and depression. Only in this way can we lead a positive life. As the proverb says, "Where there is a will, there is a way."

**范文**：

First and foremost, with the development of society, more young people choose to study or work far from home. For example, many Chinese students go abroad to study. ## 🎯 范文 6：2015 手机时代

**范文**：

As is vividly depicted in the picture, a group of people are sitting around a table, but everyone is looking at their mobile phones instead of communicating with each other. The picture reveals a thought-provoking phenomenon: mobile phones are changing our lives.

First and foremost, mobile phones have become an essential part of our daily lives. For example, we use them to communicate, work, and entertain. Only in this way can we have a healthy relationship with technology. As the proverb says, "Technology is a good servant but a bad master."
